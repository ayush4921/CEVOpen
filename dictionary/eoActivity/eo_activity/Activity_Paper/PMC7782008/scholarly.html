We reported the complete chloroplast genome sequences of Rhus potaninii which was characterized by de novo assembly with Illumina sequencing data. The size of R. potaninii complete chloroplast genome is 159,620â€‰bp in length and includes a large single copy region of 87,722â€‰bp, a small single copy region of 18,948â€‰bp, and a pair of inverted repeats of 26,475â€‰bp.
