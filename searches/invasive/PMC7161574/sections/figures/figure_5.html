Effects of pre-inoculation with F. oxysporum,F. fujikuroi alone and a mixture of both on growth parameters of I. cairica infected with C. gloeosporioides. (A) Stem length. (B) Biomass. (C) Lesion area ratio. Each value is the mean ± standard error of five replicates per treatment. Error bars indicate standard errors.
