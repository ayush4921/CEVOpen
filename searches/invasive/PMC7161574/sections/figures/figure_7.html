Effects of pre-inoculation with F. oxysporum F. fujikuroi alone and a mixture of both on hormone content and NPR1 expression in leaves of I. cairica infected with C. gloeosporioides. (A) Salicylic acid (SA) content. (B) Jasmonic acid (JA) content. (C) Non-expressor of pathogenesis-related genes-1 ( NPR1) expression . Each value is the mean ± standard error of three replicates per treatment. Error bars indicate standard errors. Different letters above error bars indicate significant difference ( P < 0.05) as determined by LSD test. FW, fresh weight.
