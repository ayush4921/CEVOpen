Effects of pre-inoculation with F. oxysporum, F. fujikuroi alone and a mixture of both on physiological characteristics of I. cairica infected with C. gloeosporioides. (A) Hydrogen peroxide (H 2O 2) concentration. (B) β-1,3-glucanase activity. (C) Chitinase activity . Each value is the mean ± standard error of four replicates per treatment.
