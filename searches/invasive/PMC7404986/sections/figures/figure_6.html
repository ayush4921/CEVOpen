Typical morphology of the mouse fibroblasts 48 h after treatment with the leaf, shoot or the root extracts of the Clermont and Toogoolawah biotypes of parthenium weed at the concentrations of 0.5 mg mL −1 in the absence or presence of UV-A radiation.
