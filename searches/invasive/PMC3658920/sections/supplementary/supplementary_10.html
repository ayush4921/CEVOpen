Additional file 1: Figure S1 Informatics pipeline used for the analysis sRNA libraries of M. oryzae different libraries. We used a pipeline with different filtering steps to analyze the raw reads obtained from Illumina. The filtering steps (left column) are summarized here and fully described in the main text. Results of each filtering step to the M. oryzae mycelial complete media (CM) library are shown in the right column, as an example. The colors of the numbers indicate either total (black) or distinct (red) sequences.
