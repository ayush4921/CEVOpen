Carbon (C), nitrogen (N), and phosphorus (P) levels and their ratios in Spartina alterniflora leaves at different tidal levels: 1, 3, and 5 km from the coastline (T1, T3, and T5, respectively). Lowercase letters represent differences at p < 0.05. TC.L: soil total C content; TN.L: leaf total N content; TP.L: leaf total P content; C/N.L: leaf C/N ratio; C/P.L: leaf C/P ratio; N/P.L: leaf N/P ratio.
