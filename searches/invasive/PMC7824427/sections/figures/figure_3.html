Soil eco-stoichiometry, organic carbon (C), and salinity characteristics at different tidal levels: 1, 3, and 5 km from the coastline (T1, T3, and T5, respectively). Lowercase letters represent differences at p < 0.05. SOC.S: soil organic C content; TC.S: total soil C content; TN.S: soil total nitrogen (N) content; TP.S: soil total phosphorus (P) content; S.S: soil salinity; C/N.S: soil C/N ratio, C means TC.S; C/P.S: soil C/P ratio, C means TC.S; N/P.S: soil N/P ratio.
