The infection rate of foliar fungus Colletotrichum sp. in growth experiment. Col+ and Col− represent seedlings with and without Colletotrichum sp., respectively. Nonparametric Mann–Whitney U tests were used to identify the difference in fungal infection rate between leaves with and without the foliar fungus Colletotrichum sp. (** represents p < 0.01). The error bar represents the standard error
