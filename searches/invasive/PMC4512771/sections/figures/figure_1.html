Biomass of competitor species in response to competition with A. petiolata in control (A) or soil with a history of A. petiolata (B). Biomass within each growth form are shown in the insets. Black bars indicate plants grown alone and grey bars indicate plants grown in competition with A. petiolata. Statistically significant differences were determined using planned orthogonal 1-df contrasts, and are indicated with an asterisk.
