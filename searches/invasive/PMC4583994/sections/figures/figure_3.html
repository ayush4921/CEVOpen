The effects of shading and a specialist herbivore ( Agasicles hygrophila) on biomass allocation of native (Argentina, dashed lines) and invasive (USA, solid lines) populations of Alternanthera philoxeroides. The allocation parameters are: root/shoot ratio,RSR; branch intensity, BI. Estimated marginal means ±1 standard error. There is no statistical significance in origin × treatment.
