The effects of shading and a specialist herbivore ( Agasicles hygrophila) on biomass of native (Argentina, dashed lines) and invasive (USA, solid lines) populations of Alternanthera philoxeroides. The biomass parameters are: total biomass and storage root biomass. Estimated marginal means ±1 standard error. Statistical significance in origin × treatment is indicated as: *p<0.05.
