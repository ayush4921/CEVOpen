Index of Germination Velocity (IGV) of ten studied species subjected to different nitrogen types and doses. The seeds were incubated at 23°C under a 14 h photoperiod for 16/8 days. Values presented are the mean (SD) (n = 4). Different letters indicate significant differences between groups (ANOVA, Bonferroni’s post-hoc test at P < 0.05).
