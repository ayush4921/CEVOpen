Light micrographs of the median nectary of B. orientalis: a Longitudinal section of the nectary showing highly vacuolated epidermal cells and intensely stained secretory parenchyma cells with numerous plastids ( arrows); staining with methylene blue-Azur B. b Numerous starch grains ( arrows) present in the secretory parenchyma cells; staining with PAS.
