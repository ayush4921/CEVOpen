Life-cycle and life-stages of Parkinsonia aculeata. Nodes represent the three main demographic stages: seed bank ( SB), juvenile ( Juv: non-reproductive) and adult ( Ad). Nodes (continuous lines) represent stages that can be stratified at an annual time step.
