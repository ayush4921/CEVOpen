Abscisic acid and gene expression. Abscisic acid content in detached leaves at 0 and 5 h ( A). Relative expression of ABA1, NCED, and PIP genes in detached leaves at 0 and 5 h ( B– D). The data are shown as means ± standard errors (SE), and the study included five biological replicates. Different letters (a, b, c, d) on the bar represent significant differences at p < 0.05 (Duncan’s test).
