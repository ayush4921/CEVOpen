Difference in plant chemicals among seven solanaceous plants. Carbon content (A), nitrogen content (B), C:N (C), soluble protein (D), soluble sugar (E), cellulose (F), lignin (G), and total phenolics (H). Data are means ± SE. Different letters show significant differences in different host plants ( p < 0.05) based on post hoc Tukey’s HSD test.
