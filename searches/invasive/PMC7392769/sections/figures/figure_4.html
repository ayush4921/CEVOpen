Mean area (km 2) estimates for current and future projections of suitable habitat, across varying climate scenarios using two climate models (HadGEM and CCSM) and across three data sources for four invasive plant species in Minnesota, USA. Public: models trained based on occurrence data obtained from public (i.e., governmental) sources; Pub + Priv: models trained on data obtained from public as well as private sources; All: models trained on all available data, regardless of reported source.
