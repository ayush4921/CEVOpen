Gas exchange parameters of S. trilobata and S. calendulacea, including the net photosynthetic rate (P n) ( A), stomatal conductance (G s) ( B), and intercellular CO 2 concentration (C i) ( C), transpiration rate (T r) ( D), and water use efficiency (WUE) ( E) (n = 5). Chlorophyll fluorescence parameters including the electron transfer rate (ETR) ( F) and actual photochemical efficiency (Φ PSII) in the leaves of the two species ( G). The error bars represent the standard deviations (SDs) of five biological replicates, and the asterisks indicate significant differences (two-sided Student’s t-test, *P < 0.05, **P < 0.01).
