Move length distributions can be described using a Pareto distribution (p( x) ~  x - μ ; panel a). Seed dispersal kernels can be described using a truncated two-dimensional (isotropic) Pareto distribution (Eq. 1; panel b). Shape parameter μ determines whether dispersal strategies are characterized by a high probability of long-distance dispersal, by balancing long- and short-distance dispersal strategies, or dominated by short-distance dispersal only
