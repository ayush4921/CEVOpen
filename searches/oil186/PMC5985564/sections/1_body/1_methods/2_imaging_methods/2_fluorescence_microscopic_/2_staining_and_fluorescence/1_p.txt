0: After the root-slices were cut, the water was removed and, based on the experiences of Fridvalszky [ 21], one to three drops of 1% aqueous solution of Nile Blue A (Carl Roth GmbH und Co. KG, Karlsruhe, Germany) were applied.
1: After incubating the root-slices for one minute at room temperature, the solution was carefully removed.
2: A drop of water was applied and the root-slices were covered with a cover glass.
3: A further incubation period of at least 30 min followed.
