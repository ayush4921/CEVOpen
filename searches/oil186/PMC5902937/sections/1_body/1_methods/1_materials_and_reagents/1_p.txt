0: The milk thistle ( Silybium marianum L.) seeds were collected from plants growing in region of Sousse (Centre of Tunisia), during April and June, 2012.
1: The seeds were authenticated at the National Botanical Research Institute Tunisia (INRAT).
2: Seeds were selected according to external color; green seeds were chosen as immature stage, mahogany brown seeds as the intermediate stage and dark brown seeds as the last stage of maturity (mature stage).
