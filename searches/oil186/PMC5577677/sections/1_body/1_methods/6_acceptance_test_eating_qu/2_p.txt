2: [ 34].
3: Three attributes (flavor, juiciness, and tenderness) were evaluated with a ten-point descriptive linear continuous scale (110) with larger scores indicating a more favorable rating.
4: Overall acceptance was rated on a nine-point hedonic scale (19) ranging from dislike extremely to like extremely.
5: Each sample assessment involved three samples that represented the nutritional treatments (NPD, LPD, and LPOB).
6: The paneling room was kept free of nonsample odors, artificial lighting was used, and the temperature was kept constant (approximately 20 C).
