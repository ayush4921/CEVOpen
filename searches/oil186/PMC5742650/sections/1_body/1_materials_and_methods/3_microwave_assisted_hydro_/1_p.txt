0: Microwave hydro-distillation has been performed using the Tecnokit Chen (Italy, Tek-2611) microwave oven ( Figure 1).
1: It is a 2450  MHz multimode microwave with a maximum delivered power of 900  W. In a typical MAHD procedure performed at atmospheric pressure, 100  gr of rosemary were heated for 30  min with addition of 300  ml water.
2: This period was sufficient to extract all the essential oils from the sample.
3: Each extraction was performed at least three times.
