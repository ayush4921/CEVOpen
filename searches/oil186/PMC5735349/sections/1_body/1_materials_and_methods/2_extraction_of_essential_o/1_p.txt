0: The leaves of  L. erythrocarpa were collected from Namwon (a region in Jeju Island, Korea) in May 2014 and LEO was extracted by hydrodistillation.
1: Briefly, approximately 300 g of fresh  L.  erythrocarpa leaves was immersed in 3 l of distilled water in a 5-l flask.
2: Subsequently, the obtained essential oil was dried over anhydrous sodium sulfate, filtered, and stored in a sealed vial at 4 C until tested.
3: The LEO yield was approximately 0.067 % (v/w).
