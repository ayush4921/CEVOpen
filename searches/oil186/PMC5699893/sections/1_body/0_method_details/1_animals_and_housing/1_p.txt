0: Sexually immature (4070 mm in length) male and female creek chub ( Semotilus atromaculatus) were used for these studies as they have been observed to express more club cells than adult.
1: Given their abundance and accessibility, the animals were wild caught.
2: Fish were housed in glass aquaria at 20C and maintained on a 12:12 light cycle.
3: Food pellets were provided daily.
4: Filters and aeration maintained water quality and fish were visually inspected daily for normal behavior and obvious signs of infection.
