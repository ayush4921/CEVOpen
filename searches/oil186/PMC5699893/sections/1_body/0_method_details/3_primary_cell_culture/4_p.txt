0: Add 10.0 mL of supplemented PBS to the pellet and vortex for 10 s at max speed to resuspend.
1: Centrifuge the cells 5000 rpm for 5 min.
2: Remove the supernatant and discard.
3: Add 10.0 mL fresh supplemented PBS.
4: Repeat the vortex/wash 2X.
5: After the final wash, remove as much PBS as possible without disrupting the pellet of cells in the bottom of the tube.
6: After the third wash, resuspend cells in 6.0 mL supplemented Leibovitz's.
