0: Forty rats were randomly divided into four groups.
1: Group 1 (control) was given corn oil (2 mL/kg, IP).
2: Group 2 (fibrosis model) was given CCl 4 1 : 4 mixture with corn oil (2.5 mL/kg, IP) twice weekly for eight weeks.
3: Rats in group 3 received CCl 4 for two weeks to establish liver injury and fibrosis and then treated with MPOE (50 mg/kg, IP), daily from the 3rd to 8th week.
4: Group 4 received MPOE (50 mg/kg, IP) from the 3rd to 8th week.
5: The selected dose for MPOE (50 mg/kg) was previously reported to be hepatoprotective for rats [ 20].
