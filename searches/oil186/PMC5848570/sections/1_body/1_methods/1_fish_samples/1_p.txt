0: Samples of  C. nigrodigitatus and  H. odoe were collected in Yabassi, Cameroon.
1: They were stored in ice and transported to the laboratory.
2: Identification was made by ichtyologists of the Laboratory of Fisheries Resources at the Institute of Fisheries and Aquatic Sciences.
3: The average lengths of these two fishes were 41.96  9.78 cm and 31.00  0.50 cm respectively for  C. nigrodigitatus and  H. odoe.
4: Their average weights were 446.06  67.67 g ( C. nigrodigitatus) and 53.57  3.10 g ( H. odoe).
