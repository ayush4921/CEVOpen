0: Twelve kilograms of each fish were washed and the undesirable parts including heads, intestines and gills were discarded.
1: Fleshes were cooked between 80 and 85 C in a household autoclave containing water on Kinderbo gas type plate for 20 min.
2: The obtained fish was pressed to separate the liquid from the solid phase.
3: The resulting pressed juice was decanted for 45 min and centrifuged 15 min at 5400 rpm/min [ 11].
4: The obtained oils were dried, weighed and stored at 4 C until use.
