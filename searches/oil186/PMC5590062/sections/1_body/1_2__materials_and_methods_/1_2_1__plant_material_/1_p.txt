0: Avicennia schaueriana Stapf & Leechm (Acanthaceae) leaves were collected at Parque Estadual da Ilha do Cardoso, Sao Paulo, Brazil (2303 to 2519 S and 4753 to 4830 W) and at Estacao Ecologica Jureia-Itatins, Sao Paulo, Brazil (2417 to 2435 S and 4700 to 4731 W), from five individual plants in each location.
1: The botanical identity was confirmed by Dr. Ines Cordeiro (Instituto de Botanica, Sao Paulo, Brazil).
2: The voucher specimens were deposited in the Herbarium of the Instituto de Botanica, Sao Paulo, Brazil, with vouchers RSCabral 16-SP for the plant collected at Parque Estadual da Ilha do Cardoso and RSCabral 46 for the one collected at Estacao Ecologica Jureia-Itatins.
