0: The leaves of  P. lentiscus were collected at the flowering stage from the mountains of Ouled Driss in Souk ahras (3621N, 807E, 800 m) during 2013.
1: The sample was dried in the shade away from light at room temperature.
2: After drying, the sample was cut to very small parts with analytical mill, used for the extraction of essential oil.
3: The identification of the investigated specie was confirmed by Laboratory of Valorization of Natural Biological Resources (Setif, Algeria), where a voucher specimen was deposited in a botanical collection.
