0: Four different samples of  Allium vineale were collected from a field in Huntsville, Alabama (343846 N, 863327 W, 191 m elevation) on 10 April 2017, 8 a.m. Each sample was cleaned of debris, the entire plant (leaves and bulbs) chopped, and hydrodistilled using a Likens-Nickerson apparatus for 4 h with continuous extraction with dichloromethane (CH 2Cl 2).
