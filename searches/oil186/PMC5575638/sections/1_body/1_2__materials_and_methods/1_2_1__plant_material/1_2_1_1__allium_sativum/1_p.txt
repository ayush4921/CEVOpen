0: Bulbs of  Allium sativum were collected from a field in Las Pedroneras, Spain (392659 N, 24023 W, 745 m elevation), in December 2015.
1: Garlic bulbs were finely chopped, and were subjected to three different distillation methods: laboratory hydrodistillation using a Clevenger apparatus for 3 h, industrial hydrodistillation for 4 h, and industrial steam distillation for 5 h. Pale yellow essential oils were obtained in 0.2%, 0.22% and 0.18% yields, respectively.
2: The obtained essential oils and hydrosol were separated by decantation; remaining water was removed from the essential oils with sodium chloride.
3: The collected essential oil samples were stored under refrigeration (4 C) until analysis.
