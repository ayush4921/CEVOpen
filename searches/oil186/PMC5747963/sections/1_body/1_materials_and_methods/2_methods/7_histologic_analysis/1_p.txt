0: After 18th day treatment, the animals were killed, full-thickness biopsies were made in correspondence of the lesion area, and histologic analysis of the excised tissues was performed.
1: A biopsy of intact skin was also taken for comparison.
2: Tissue samples were bisected along the widest line of the wound, fixed for 48 h in 4% w/v neutral buffered paraformaldehyde, dehydrated with gradient ethanol series, cleared in xylene, and embedded in paraffin.
3: Sections (8 m) were obtained using a Leitz microtome (Wetzlar, Germany) and were stained with hematoxylin and eosin (H&E).
4: The slices were examined at 5 magnification under a light microscope (Zeiss Axiophot, Carl Zeiss, Jena, Germany), equipped with a digital camera.
