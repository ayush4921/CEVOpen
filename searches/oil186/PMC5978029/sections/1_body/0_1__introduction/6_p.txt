2: Basil oils ( Ocimum basilicum) exhibit antibacterial activity against  Brochothrix thermosphacta,  E. coli,  L. innocua,  L. monocytogenes,  P. putida,  S. typhimurium,  S. putrefaciens and  M. flavus microorganisms [ 8].
