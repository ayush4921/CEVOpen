0: Statistical evaluation of the data was performed with the program GraphPad, San Diego California, USA, version 5.0.
1: The statistical test used was ANOVA for multiple comparisons, followed by the Bonferroni test.
2: Values of p<0.01 were considered statistically significant and are indicated by an asterisk.
3: Each MIC experiment and indirect biofilm biomass quantification was performed with five replicates, and the CBM tests and CFU counts were performed using three replicates.
4: All tests were performed in three independent experiments.
