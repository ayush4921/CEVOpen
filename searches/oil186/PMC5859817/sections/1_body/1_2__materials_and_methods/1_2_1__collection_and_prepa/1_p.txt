0: Cleistocalyx operculatus leaves were purchased from local herbal supplier in Go Vap District, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam (Thanh Binh Medicinal Plants and Herbals Co., Vietnam).
1: Air-dried and ground leaves were subjected to hydrodistillation for 4 hours at 100C in 15% NaCl solution using a Clevenger apparatus [ 20].
2: The essential oils were collected over aqueous phase amd transferred into 1.5 mL tube, after which essential oil was stored in dark chamber at 4C prior to GC/MS analysis and bioactivities testing.
