0: The aerial parts of oregano were collected in early summer 2014, near the town of Yazd (Yazd Province, Iran).
1: The plant materials were identified by Dr Seyed Mansour Mirtadzadini, a botanist at Shahid Bahonar University of Kerman, Kerman, Iran.
2: The aerial parts of the plant were dried at room temperature and the essential oil (EO) was extracted by hydrodistillation for approximately 3 h using a Clevenger-type apparatus.
