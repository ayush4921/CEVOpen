0: Anisakis simplex L3 larvae were isolated from the intermediary host  Micromesistius poutassou (Risso) (blue whiting) purchased from the fishmonger in Villanueva de Gallego (Zaragoza, Spain).
1: The worms were washed several times on sterile solution of 0.9% NaCl and identified under light microscope according to morphological features.
