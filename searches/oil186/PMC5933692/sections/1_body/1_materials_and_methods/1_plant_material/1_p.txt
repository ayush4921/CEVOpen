0: Branches with leaves of  G.  rosmarinifolia were collected in February 2017 from plants located in the following site: Raffo Rosso, Monte Cuccio, and Vallone Sagana (381107N 131605 ESicilyPalermo).
1: The matrices were placed in paper bags and kept at 4  1 C for 24 hours before the hydrodistillation.
2: A voucher specimen (N MS 120/2017) was deposited at the Herbarium of the University of Palermo.
3: No specific permits were required for the described location and for the collection of plant material.
4: The plant species used in the present study is not endangered or protected.
