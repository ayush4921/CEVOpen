0: The essential oil of  P. tobira seeds was extracted by HD using a Clevenger-type apparatus.
1: Briefly, 50 g of fine powder of  P. tobira were immersed in 500 mL of distilled water and extracted during 3 h. The distilled essential oils were separated using hexane, dried over anhydrous sodium sulfate and kept in a dark glass bottle at 4 C until further analysis.
