0: The shade-dried powdered leaves (1 kg) of  O. vulgare were soaked in ethanol (95%) for 4 days.
1: After 4 days, the ethanolic layer was decanted off.
2: The process was repeated for four times.
3: The solvent from the total extract was distilled off, and the concentrate was evaporated on a water bath to a syrupy consistency and then evaporated to dryness.
