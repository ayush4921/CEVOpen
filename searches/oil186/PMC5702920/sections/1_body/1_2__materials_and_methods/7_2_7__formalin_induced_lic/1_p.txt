0: The peripheral analgesic and anti-inflammatory activities were evaluated using a model adopted by Hunskaar and Hole [ 26].
1: Immediately after inhalation, animals were withdrawn from the chamber and 20  L of a 2.5% (v/v) formalin solution in PBS was injected subcutaneously into the right hind paw.
2: Animals were then transferred to a transparent acrylic box subdivided into six equal square areas.
3: The time that mice licked their right hind paw in the first five minutes and from 15 to 30 minutes following completion of the inhalation test was timed.
