0: N. sativa powder of 10 g was measured by mean of digital scale (Shimadzu, Tokyo, Japan) and placed in flask.
1: A total of 50 mL of solvent was added to each flask.
2: The top of the flasks was covered by aluminum foil.
3: The flasks were shacked using Shaking Incubator (Heidolph Unimax 1010, Heidolph Instruments GmbH & Co. KG Walpersdorfer, Schwabach, Germany) at 57 rpm for 4 hours.
4: The extracts were filtered by One Man paper (no 1) twice.
5: The extracts were evaporated by means of rotatory evaporator (Heidolph Laborota 4000, Germany).
6: The yields were measured by digital scale (Shimazu) and protected from light by aluminum foil.
7: The oils were kept in a refrigerator (80C) for further analysis.
