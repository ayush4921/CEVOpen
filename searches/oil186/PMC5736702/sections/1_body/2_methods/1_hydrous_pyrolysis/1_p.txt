0: Hydrous pyrolysis followed the method of Lewan 44,  45.
1: The crushed rock samples (24 g, 13 mm maximum size) were loaded into gas chromatograph ovens and isothermally heated for 72 h, plus ~510 min warm-up.
2: Rock residues were rinsed in acetone at experiment conclusion to remove generated bitumens from surfaces.
3: Residues were vacuum-dried overnight prior to preparation for petrographic and AFM-IR analysis.
