0: Matured, fresh conophor nuts were obtained from a market near the Federal University of Technology Akure, Nigeria.
1: The nuts were washed to remove dirt and divided into three parts.
2: A part was boiled at 100C for 30 min and another portion was toasted at 120C for 45 min, while the third part was used in the raw form.
3: All the nuts were shelled separately and comminuted using mortar and pestle to obtain coarse flour, which were then further ground in a mill (Retsch, Germany) and kept at 4C for further analyses.
