0: Six- to eight-week-old female BALB/c mice were obtained from the Central Animal Facility of the Federal University of Pelotas, where the immunization assays were also conducted.
1: All mice were kept in cages containing wood shavings and bedding and had free access to water and a maintenance diet  ad libitum.
2: A 12-h light/dark cycle at room temperature (21  2 C) was also maintained.
3: All experiments were performed in compliance with the procedures of the Brazilian College of Animal Experimentation (COBEA).
4: The Ethics Commission on Animal Experimentation (CEEA) of Pelotas Federal University (UFPel) approved the project (number 2422).
5: All efforts were made to minimize animal suffering.
