0: Red propolis was collected from the municipality of Brejo Grande, located in the state of Sergipe, in northeastern Brazil (S 1028'25 and W 3626'12).
1: Samples were collected in September 2011 and were maintained frozen at 20 C until use.
2: For the preparation of the essential oil, conventional hydrodistillation was carried out using a Clevenger-type apparatus.
3: Briefly, 380 g of BRP was transferred to a volumetric flask, and 2 L of distilled water was added.
4: A heating mantle was placed onto the Clevenger apparatus.
5: The essential oil was extracted after boiling the mixture for a time period of one hour.
6: The essential oil obtained was collected in amber vials and stored at 4 C until use.
7: The total yield obtained was approximately 0.25%.
