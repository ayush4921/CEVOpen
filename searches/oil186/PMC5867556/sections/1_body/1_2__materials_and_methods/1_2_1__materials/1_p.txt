0: Finished samples from the same poplar propolis extract were prepared by B Natural (Corbetta, Milano, Italy,  http://www.bnatural.it/) consisting of one powder product named ESIT12 and four liquid extracts.
1: ESIT12 is a micronized sample composed of propolis with a minimum of 12% total polyphenols supported by sucrose and silicon dioxide.
2: The four liquid samples were prepared by B Natural in hydroalcoholic (80% ethanol/20% water), glycolic (98% propylene glycol with the remaining 2% of ethanol), and glyceric (95% and 5% water) solutions, and oil (100% seed oil).
