0: The gas chromatograph was a Shimadzu GC 2010 (Shimadzu Corp., Columbia, MD, USA) equipped with a flame ionization detector, a split/splitless injector, and autosampler AOC-20i (Shimadzu Corp., Columbia, MD, USA).
1: The capillary column was a ZB-5MS (Phenomenex Inc., Torrance, CA, USA) with a film thickness of 0.25 m. The column temperature was programmed, 50250 C at 2 C/min, the injector temperature was 250 C, the detector temperature was 280 C, the carrier gas was nitrogen, and the flow rate was maintained at 1.0 mL/min.
2: Injection mode split with a split ratio of 1:100.
3: The injected volume was 0.3 L of diluted oil (1:10  v/ v with CH 2Cl 2).
4: The percent composition of the oleoresin essential oil was calculated from raw peak areas without standardization.
