0: The leaf, stem, seed, rhizome, and pod of  A. danielli were collected from mature plants cultivated in Uyo Local Government Area of Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria, in the month of March, 2016.
1: The sample was identified by a taxonomist in the Department of Botany and Ecological Studies, University of Uyo, where the voucher specimen was deposited.
2: The essential oils were obtained by hydro-distillation (4 h) of the fresh plant parts using a Clevenger-type apparatus (Garg Process Glass, India Pvt.
3: Ltd., Mumbai, India) in accordance with the British Pharmacopoeia [ 17].
4: The oils were dried over sodium sulfate (Sigma-Aldrich, St. Louis, MO, USA) and stored in refrigeration (4 C) after the estimation of percentage yield.
