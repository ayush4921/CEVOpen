0: Fatty acid methyl esters (FAME) were prepared according to AOCS method Ce 1K-07 [ 14].
1: FAME were analyzed by gas chromatography (Agilent 6890, USA).
2: The apparatus was equipped with a flame ionisation detector and a BPX70 column (60 m length, 0.32 mm internal diameter, and 0.25  m film; SGE Europe).
3: Helium was used as the carrier gas at a flow rate of 1 ml/min.
4: The temperature of the column oven was 170C.
5: The temperature of the injector and the detector was 220C.
