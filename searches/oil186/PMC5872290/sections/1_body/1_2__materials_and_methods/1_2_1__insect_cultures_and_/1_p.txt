0: Larvae of  C. quinquefasciatus were collected from water tanks in the Sanctorum Cemetery in Mexico City, Mexico (192717 N, 991247 W).
1: Identification of adults and larvae was done based on Harwood and James descriptions [ 20].
2: Mosquitoes larvae were pooled into groups of 50 individuals of first and second instars in glass bottles containing distilled water.
3: Afterwards, larvae were maintained at 26  2 C with a natural photoperiod and supplied with powdered mixture of dog food and baking powder (3:1).
4: The third instar emerging larvae were then separated into groups of 10 individuals in 100 mL tubes containing distilled water.
