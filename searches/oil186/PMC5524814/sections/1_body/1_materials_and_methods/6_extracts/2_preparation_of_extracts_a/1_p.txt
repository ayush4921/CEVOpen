0: Two types of extracts were obtained.
1: Powdered air-dried plant material (0.5 g) was sonicated for 15 min with either 5 mL of ethanol/water (50/50, v/v) or 5 mL of dichloromethane.
2: After centrifugation for 10 min, the extracts were filtered through a membrane filter of 45 m and the supernatants, which were used as test solutions, were stored at 4C till used.
