0: Precultures were made in 2 mL SC-U medium with 2% (w/v) raffinose and 1% (w/v) tergitol NP-40 (Sigma, USA), and were grown overnight at 30C.
1: For induction gene expression, appropriate amount of precultures were inoculated into 20 mL SC-U medium containing 2% (w/v) galactose and 50 M linoleic acid at an OD 600 of 0.1.
2: Yeast cultures were incubated for three days at 30C under continuous agitation (250 rpm).
3: Then the cells were harvested and washed twice with sterile deionized water before used for fatty acid analysis.
