0: At the time of sacrifice, the liver and kidney tissues were removed and fixed in 10% formaldehyde solution and washed.
1: The tissues were dehydrated in increasing gradient of ethanol, finally cleared in toluene and embedded in molten paraffin wax.
2: Sections were cut at 45 m thickness and stained with hematoxylin and eosin (H&E).
3: The slides were photographed with an Olympus UTU1X-2 camera connected to an Olympus CX41 microscope (Tokyo, Japan).
