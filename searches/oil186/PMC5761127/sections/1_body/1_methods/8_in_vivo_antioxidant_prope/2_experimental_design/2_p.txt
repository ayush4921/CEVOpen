0: The rats of group 1 served as normal control and received saline orally daily for 7 days and were injected with 1 ml/kg BW of just olive oil (the solvent of CCl 4) on the 7 day.
1: The rats of group 2 served as CCl 4-hepato and renotoxicity control and were received saline orally daily for 7 days and were injected with 1 ml/kg BW of CCl 4 and olive oil mixture on the 7 day (a single intraperitoneal injection).
2: The CCl 4 dose was selected according to the reference dose for chronic oral exposure (RFD) as recommended for CCl 4 (CASRN 5623-5) [ 21].
