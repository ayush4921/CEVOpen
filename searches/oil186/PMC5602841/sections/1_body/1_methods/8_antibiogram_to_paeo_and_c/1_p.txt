0: The antimicrobial activity of PAEO and its major component was performed from the disk diffusion test on Mueller Hinton Agar, as detailed on the Antibiogram item.
1: Sterile paper discs (6 mm in diameter) were soaked with PAEO (20 L) and 98% Carvacrol (20 L) (Sigma).
2: The antimicrobial effect was detected by the formation of inhibition zones around the disks soaked with PAEO and carvacrol.
3: The halos were measured in millimeters, labeled as bioactive those larger than 15 mm.
4: As positive and negative control, tetracycline (30 g) and disks soaked with 20 LTween 80 solution 3% were used, respectively.
