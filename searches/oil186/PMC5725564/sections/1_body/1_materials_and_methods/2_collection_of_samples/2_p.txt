0: The fruits of blood plum ( Haematostaphis barteri) were purchased from a local market in Zing local government area, Taraba State, Nigeria.
1: The fruits were thoroughly sorted to remove the stones, and the bad ones before washing with tap water.
2: The clean fruits were then transported to the laboratory and proper identification was made by a Taxonomist in the Department of Biological Sciences, Federal University Wukari, Taraba State, Nigeria.
