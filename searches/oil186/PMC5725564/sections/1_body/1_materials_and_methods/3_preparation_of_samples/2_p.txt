0: Fifty number clean fruits of  H. barteri were dried in an oven at 40C for 5 days in order to separate the pulp from the seed because the pulp is thin and juicy.
1: The pulp was separated from the seed, and freely ground with Kenwood food blender.
2: The seed was also carefully de-shelled using kitchen knife, and the kernel was dried in an oven at 45C for 36h and ground into powder.
3: Flours of the two samples were separately kept in the refrigerator at -4C prior to use.
