0: For sensory analysis, meats were removed from deep freeze 24 h prior to use, thawed at + 4 C and grilled.
1: Panelists assessed the odor, tenderness, taste, juiciness, appearance and overall acceptability for grilled meat samples scoring from 1 to 5.
2: The assessors were selected and trained according to the international standards [ 23].
3: The sensory evaluation was performed according to a standardized sensory descriptive method [ 24].
