0: Fresh sage ( Salvia officinalis, Jacobs Farm organic sage, Pescadero, CA, USA, grown in Mexico) was purchased from a local market in Huntsville, Alabama on 8 April 2017.
1: The fresh leaves (34.64 g) were chopped and hydrodistilled using a LikensNickerson apparatus for 4 h with continuous extraction with dichloromethane (CH 2Cl 2) to give 1.653 g yellow essential oil.
2: Commercial sage leaf essential oils were obtained from Mountain Rose Herbs (Eugene, OR; oil from California) and Selikaj Ltd. (Koplik, Albania).
