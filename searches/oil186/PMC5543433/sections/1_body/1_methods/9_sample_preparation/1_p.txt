0: Raw meat beef was provided by a local supplier (slaughterhouse of Sfax, Tunisia).
1: They were placed in insulated polystyrene boxes on ice and transferred to the laboratory within 1 h of slaughtering.
2: Raw meat beef was raw minced by grinding in a sterile grinder.
3: Concerning the conditioning of raw minced beef for storage at 4 C studies, five equal portions (C, T1- T4): C (Control), T 1 ( ClEO: 0.06%), T 2 ( ClEO: 0.312%), T 3 (BHT at 0.01%), T 4 ( ClEO 0.312% and BHT at 0.01%).
4: For each experiment, all runs were applied to a single batch of raw minced beef meat.
5: Each formulation produced one mixture homogeneous which was vacuum stuffed into plastic casings to produce three replicates.
