0: The  P. zopfii isolates were cultured in Sabouraud Dextrose Agar (bioMerieux, Poland) and incubated at 37 C for 48 h under aerobic conditions.
1: Algae suspensions were prepared with an optical density of 0.5 on the McFarland scale using a densitometer (Den-1B McFarland BioSan Densitometer).
2: Antibiotic susceptibility was investigated by the disk diffusion method on Yeast Nitrogen Base Agar (Becton Dickinson and Company, USA) or on Mueller-Hinton II Agar (Graso, Poland).
3: The following antifungal chemotherapeutic agents were used (Graso, Poland): nystatin (100 g), amphotericin B (20 g), clotrimazole (10 g), miconazole (10 g), ketoconazole (10 g), fluconazole (25 g), econazole (10 g), flucytosine (1 g).
4: The results were verified after 24 and 48 h of incubation at 37 C for all antimicrobials.
5: The inhibitory zone diameters were measured and interpreted according to Clinical and Laboratory Standard Institute (CLSI) [ 21] and EUCAST standards [ 22].
