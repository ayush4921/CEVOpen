0: Samples of  C. citriodora leaves and  C. macrocarpa branchlets were cut into small pieces (100 g) and hydro-distillated for 3 h, in a Clevenger apparatus [ 40].
1: The oil was collected and the mass of fresh weight of sample was measured (3.15 and 4.70 mL/100 g fresh weight, from  C. citriodora and  C. macrocarpa, respectively).
2: The oil was kept dry in sealed Eppendorf tubes and stored at 4 C prior for chemical analysis.
