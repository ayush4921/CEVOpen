0: The ethanolic extract was obtained by Soxhlet extraction of ground leaves of  Ocimum micranthum Willd, using reagent-grade ethanol (JT Baker) as the solvent.
1: The ethanol was recovered through Buchi rotary evaporator (model R-215) with a vacuum controller (V-850) coupled to a cooling unit.
2: The extract was stored in glass bottles and then filtered through a filtration system comprising a stainless-steel base and a Millipore filter (0.22 m).
3: Finally, the ethanolic extract was labeled and refrigerated at 4 C until its analysis.
