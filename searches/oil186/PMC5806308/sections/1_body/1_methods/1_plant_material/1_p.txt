0: The  Ocimum micranthum Willd was taxonomically classified and identified by the biologist Jose L. Tapia of the Herbarium at Natural Resources Unit of the Center for Scientific Research of Yucatan, Merida, Yucatan, Mexico.
1: A specimen was deposited in this same Herbarium with reference number 68785.
2: Leaves of this plant were used to obtain the extracts evaluated in the present study.
3: Leaves were collected during the winter season between December 2013 and February 2014 at 100 m around the point 219'10.91" North latitude and 895'4.58" West longitude in the town of Cansahcab, Yucatan, Mexico.
4: Harvested leaves were washed and then dried in a convection oven at 50 C for 16 h. Finally, the leaves were ground in a mill Ika (model A11).
