0: H. persicum L. is a perennial herb that usually grows up to 1.52 m. This alternateleaved plant has an aniselike smell with thick and hollow stem.
1: The leaf blades are elongated, densely haired on the lower side, glabrous on top, and pinnate with blunttoothed margins.
2: The flowers with five petals and five stamens are small with pale white and limegreen color.
3: The fruits are broadly obovate, with slightly ridged schizocarp (Majidi & Sadati Lamardi,  2018).
