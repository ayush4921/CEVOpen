Effect of soil pollution with crude oil (given on X-axis in %) on ( a) hydrogen peroxide content and ( b) malondialdehyde (MDA) content in shoots of two rye varieties. Different lower-case letters indicate significant differences between plants by oil concentration in the soil for each variety separately, and asterisks * indicate significant differences between rye varieties at p ≤ 0.05 based on post hoc Tukey’s test ( n = 4).
