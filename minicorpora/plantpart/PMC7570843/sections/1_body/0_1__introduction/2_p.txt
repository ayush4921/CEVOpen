0: Essential oils are the volatile fraction of the secondary metabolites produced by plants and have a specific narcotic odor, are colorless, highly refractive and easily inflammable.
1: Such volatile constituents are mainly a mixture of terpenes and terpenoids.
2: Monoterpenes are the most representative essential oils molecules constituting 90% of the total.
3: However, as essential oils are highly complex, they may also include oxygenated compounds such as alcohols, aldehydes, ketones, acids, phenols, oxides, lactones, acetals, ethers and esters [ 5].
