0: The use of plants and their essential oils for therapeutic purposes has been extremely popular since ancient times.
1: Essential oils of various plants appear to have a variety of applications, such as analgesic, antibacterial, antimicrobial, antioxidant, and anticancer applications.
2: They are used by many industries and find applications in agriculture, where they are mainly applied to organic crops as insecticides; in cosmetics to create beauty products; in the sanitary products industry for food preservation and household cleaners; in the food industry to increase the flavor and taste of food; and even in embalmment.
3: Due to the variety of attributed beneficial properties, recent research has turned its interest to studying the bioactivities of aromatic plants and their extracts and further dissecting the involved mechanisms of action [ 1, 2, 3, 4].
