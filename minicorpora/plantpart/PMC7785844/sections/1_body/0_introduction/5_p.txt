1: Research investigations have revealed the plant as a potential source of pharmaceutical products.
2: Miracle fruit is popular among patients with diabetes and obesity in Japan.
3: A mixture of miracle fruit, carambola, pumpkin and papaya could boost the immune function of mice ( Du et al., 2014).
4: In view of the uniqueness of this plant which enjoys appreciable mention in scientific literature, the present study aims to present a holistic review that collates scientific information on its ethnomedicinal uses, phytochemical constituents, biological activities and nutritional benefits.
