0: Bed bugs ( Cimex lectularius L.) are blood-sucking ectoparasites that usually feed at night [ 1].
1: During daytime, bed bugs remain hidden in cracks and crevices around sleeping and resting areas of its host.
2: Bed bugs become highly active after the onset of darkness in search for a host [ 2] which they locate following visual, olfactory, and thermal signals that are detected by the sensory system located primarily on the insects antenna [ 3].
3: It is during this time that insects might encounter semiochemicals and chemical insecticides.
