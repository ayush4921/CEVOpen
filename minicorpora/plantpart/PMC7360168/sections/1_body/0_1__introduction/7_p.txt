0: This work is part of the research and development of bioactive substances such as natural substances with antioxidant activity of interest in biopharmacology.
1: The main objective of this work is to assess  in vitro the essential oil antioxidant activity of the aerial part of  Tetraclinis articulata (Vahl) using the method of DPPH free radical trapping and the antibacterial activity on three strains:  Staphylococcus aureus,  Pseudomonas aeruginosa, and  Escherichia coli.
