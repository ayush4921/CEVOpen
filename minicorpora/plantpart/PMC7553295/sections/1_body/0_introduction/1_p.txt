0: Most helminth parasites are broadly categorized into two main phyla, namely, nematodes (roundworms) including intestinal and filarial worms, and the Platyhelminthes (flatworms) including the flukes (trematodes) and tapeworms (cestodes) [ 1].
1: Helminthic parasite infections receive less than one percent of global research funding and therefore, are considered as neglected tropical diseases [ 2].
2: About 1/3 of the 3 billion people in the developing regions of the Americas, sub-Saharan Africa, and Asia living in low socioeconomic conditions are infected with one or more helminths [ 1].
