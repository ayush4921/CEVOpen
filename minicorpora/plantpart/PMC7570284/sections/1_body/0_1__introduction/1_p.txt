0: Mosquito-borne infectious diseases have been a persistent problem in Vietnam.
1: Dengue fever and dengue hemorrhagic fever are especially problematic and chikungunya fever is an emerging threat in the country [ 1, 2].
2: Aedes aegypti (L.) (Diptera: Culicidae), the yellow fever mosquito, and  Aedes albopictus (Skuse) (Diptera: Culicidae), the Asian tiger mosquito, are important vectors of several viral pathogens, including dengue fever virus [ 3], yellow fever virus [ 4], chikungunya fever virus [ 5], and possibly Zika virus [ 6].
3: Culex quinquefasciatus Say (Diptera: Culicidae), the southern house mosquito, is a vector of lymphatic filariasis [ 7] as well as several arboviruses such as West Nile virus and St. Louis encephalitis virus [ 8], and possibly Zika virus [ 9].
