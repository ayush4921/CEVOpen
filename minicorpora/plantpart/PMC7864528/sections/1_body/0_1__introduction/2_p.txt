0: Incorporation of trehalose into food packaging is suggested for diverse purposes.
1: It helps to preserve the aroma and color of dried fruits and improve weight loss and color of packaged vegetables, but no differences in firmness were found between coated and uncoated samples [ 7, 8].
2: Trehalose addition to food packaging material can also prevent a reduction in organic acid content during storage [ 9].
3: Another biological advantage of trehalose is its capability to improve plant cells viability after freezing and subsequent thawing [ 10].
4: Furthermore, the results of the previous works indicated that trehalose also possesses UV barrier properties [ 11].
