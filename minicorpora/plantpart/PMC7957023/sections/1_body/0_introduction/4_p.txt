0: There are also economic reasons to reformulate chocolate products.
1: Due to the recent increase in demand for these items, the cost of cocoa powder and CB has become higher: in 2019 alone, the price of CB increased by 4% in the USA and 5% in Europe ( International Cocoa Organ, 2019).
2: Hence, reducing or replacing CB in chocolate is essential to develop products with improved manufacturing costs and more convenient selling prices.
