0: Recently, it has been shown that a variety of active components of traditional Chinese medicine regulate the body's immune state to attenuate inflammation [ 9 11].
1: Volatile Chinese medicine oils are mixtures of multiple compounds [ 12].
2: The main components are monoterpene and sesquiterpene, among others, with anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, acaricidal, antiviral, sedative, antianxiety, and antidepression properties [ 13].
3: The anti-inflammatory effect of volatile oils of traditional Chinese medicine is mainly realized through the regulation of cyclooxygenase (COX) and induction of nitric oxide synthase (iNOS) and a variety of cytokines, which play roles in the inflammation process [ 14].
4: In recent years, essential oils of traditional Chinese medicine have been described as potential sources for the development of new drugs.
