0: Within the Lamiaceae, the Mints ( Mentha) are of economical relevance, and have a long history of human use as aromatic and medical compounds.
1: This genus comprises around 30 species of aromatic perennial herbs, distributed mostly in temperate and sub-temperate regions of Eurasia, Australia, Africa, and North America ( Dorman et al., 2003;  Kanatt et al., 2007;  Hui et al., 2010).
2: Recently, the chemical diversity of this genus has been made accessible by genomics defining a repertory of metabolic genes that have contributed to the different chemotypes ( Mint Evolutionary Genomics Consortium, 2018).
