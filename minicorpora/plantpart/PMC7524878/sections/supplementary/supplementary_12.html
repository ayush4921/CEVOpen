Supplementary Figure 3

Response of actin filaments to essential oil from A. rugosa (A, B) or menthone/isomenthone (C, D) for either 5 min (A, C), or 30 min (B, D), respectively. Inaction was visualized by the actin-binding domain of plant fimbrin and followed under the spinning disc microscope. Cells were treated at day 3 after sub-cultivation.
