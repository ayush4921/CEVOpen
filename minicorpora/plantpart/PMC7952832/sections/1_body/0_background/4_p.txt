0: Recently, the governor of Oyo State in Nigeria revealed that the oil from  N. sativa seed was one of the remedies he consumed to combat the disease, a week after he tested positive for COVID-19 (Punch Newspaper, April 7, 2020).
1: So far, no attempt has been made to investigate the veracity of this claim.
2: In this study, the oil from the seed of  N. sativa was extracted, and the identified phytochemical components docked on some selected SARS-CoV-2 proteins.
3: The dock scores of these compounds were compared to the values obtained with a standard COVID-19 prescription drug.
