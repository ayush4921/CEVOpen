1: The composition of the essential oil of  P. dioica leaves from Jamaica showed presence of eugenol, -pinene, caryophyllene, limonene, and 1,8-cineole [ 3]; while eugenol (85.33%), -caryophyllene (4.36%), cineole (4.19%), linalool (0.83%) and -humulene (0.76%) were present in the essential oil of  P. dioica leaves from Sri Lanka [ 10].
2: Eugenol (76.20%), -myrcene (10.70%), and ( E)-caryophyllene (4.20%) were present in the essential oil from  P. dioica leaves from Guatemala [ 9]; and a supercritical fluid extract of leaves of  P.dioica from Mexico presented eugenol (77.90%) as the major component [ 11].
