0: Several reviews of plant lignans are available [ 17, 40, 41, 42].
1: Because of their nutritional importance, information about food lignans has been collected in several databases [ 43].
2: As these databases and reviews covered lignans from many plant species, space devoted to lignans of sesame was limited.
3: In 2013, Dar and Arumugam [ 44] published a review entitled Lignans of sesame: purification methods, biological activities and biosynthesisa review but they, too, covered lignans from many plants while the coverage of sesame was limited.
4: A comprehensive review focusing on the lignans of sesame has been missing.
5: This work attempts to review chemical, biological, and applied aspects of sesame lignans comprehensively.
6: The literature was surveyed from the first reports on sesame lignans in the 1890th, including scarcely accessible historical texts.
7: Representative examples were selected for finding documented in many reports.
8: Controversial issues were critically examined, and several errors perpetuated in literature were corrected.
