0: Mosquito-borne infectious diseases have been a continuous health problem in Southeast Asia, including Vietnam.
1: Dengue fever and dengue hemorrhagic fever are particularly problematic and chikungunya fever is an emerging threat in the country [ 1, 2].
2: Aedes aegypti (L.) (Diptera: Culicidae), the yellow fever mosquito, is a recognized vector of dengue fever virus, chikungunya fever virus, Zika virus, and yellow fever virus [ 3].
3: Aedes albopictus (Skuse) (Diptera: Culicidae), the Asian tiger mosquito, is a key vector of several pathogenic viruses, including yellow fever virus [ 4], dengue fever virus [ 5], chikungunya virus [ 6], and possibly Zika virus [ 7].
4: Culex quinquefasciatus Say (Diptera: Culicidae), the southern house mosquito, is a vector of lymphatic filariasis [ 8] as well as several arboviruses such as West Nile virus and St. Louis encephalitis virus [ 9] and possibly Zika virus [ 10].
