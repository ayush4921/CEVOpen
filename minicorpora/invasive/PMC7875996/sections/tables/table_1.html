GI (Gall Index) on a scale of 0–5; RF (Reproduction Factor) = Pf/Pi where Pf = final population and Pi = initial population; Resistance degree (RD): R = resistant (GI ≤ 2 and RF ≤ 1); S = Susceptible (GI > 2 and RF > 1); aControls. Data from five plants per treatment of two independent experiments (n = 10) were submitted to ANOVA, and comparison of means by LSD test (P < 0.05) was carried out for GI and RF. Values with the same symbol are not significantly different.
