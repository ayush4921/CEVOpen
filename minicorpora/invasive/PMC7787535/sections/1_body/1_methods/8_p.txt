0: Each of the facilitators was from the initial panel of six experts who created the original participant invitiation list.
1: They conducted the facilitation by first restating the initial question on the shortlist, then asking for clarifications or amendements, then asking how the questions should be ranked.
2: The last point involved placing questions into categories of gold, silver and bronze.
3: Gold questions were defined as suitable, compelling and likely to make it into the final 100.
4: Silver questions were designated as generally suitable and have a decent potential to make it into the final 100.
5: Bronze questions were described as lower quality but still show the promise to be advanced into the final 100.
