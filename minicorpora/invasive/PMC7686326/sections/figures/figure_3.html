Overview of Baringo lowlands (Njemps Flats) in Kenya, the location of the sampled plots and sample photos for each land cover type. The large map (middle), is displayed on a digital elevation model generated by the Shuttle Radar Topography Mission (SRTM), provided by United States Geological Survey (USGS) available at https://earthexplorer.usgs.gov/. The two inset maps on the left for Kenya and Africa were generated using GIS data downloaded from World Resources Institute ( www.wri.org) and ESRI ( www.arcgis.com) respectively. The map was designed using ArcMap version 10.2.2.
