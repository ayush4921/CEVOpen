Enriched GO terms in M vsNIM and R vsNIR of A. ostoyae for transcriptomics ( A) and proteomics ( B). Grey dots represent GO terms, enriched in only one of the comparisons i.e. either mycelium vs. non-invasive mycelium or rhizomorphs vs. non-invasive rhizomorphs.
