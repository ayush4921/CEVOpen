The effects of different subsurface extracts on the growth of A. philoxeoides. ( A) stem length and ( B) node number of ramets and ( C) leaf number of ramets and ( D) leaf area and ( E) root length and ( F) root number of A. phlioxeoldes (from left to right, the columns are control, Petroleum ether (PE), ethyl acetate (EE) and n-Butanol (NE)). The bars in the figure stand for the Std. Deviation of the replications (n = 5). The treatments with the same letter are not significantly difference at 0.05 level.
