0: The experiment followed a completely randomized design, composed of three extracts (petroleum ether, ethyl acetate, and n-butanol) of  A. philoxeroides.
1: Data were tested for normality and homogeneity of variance.
2: ANOVA was conducted on morphology and physiological indices with exudate treatments for  A. philoxeroides.
3: Differences between means were assessed with LSDs and Duncans test ( P < 0.05), using the SPSS v.21.0 for Windows.
4: Graphs are performed by Sigmaplot 12.0.
