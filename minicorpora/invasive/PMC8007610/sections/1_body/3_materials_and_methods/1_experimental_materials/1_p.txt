0: Plants of  A. philoxeroides and  H. scandens were collected from the campus of Anhui Agriculture University in China (N3152, E11716).
1: Fresh  H. scandens roots were dried to constant weight under shade, and ground to fine powder passing through 40 mesh sieve, then put in a desiccator.
2: The  A. philoxeroides plants were cut into several cuttings, each with one node.
3: The regents 95% ethanol, petroleum ether, ethyl acetate, and n-butanol were analytical pure, purchased from Jinan Century Tongda Chemical Co., Ltd.
