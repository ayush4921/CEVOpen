0: Climate in the region is semiarid and temperate, with average annual precipitation of 574.4 mm (precipitation is seasonally distributed, with 60% falling in autumn-winter) and an average annual temperature of 8.6C (non-published data from the meteorological station on San Ramon ranch).
1: Strong W-NW winds are frequent all year round, with a mean speed of 30 km/h [ 46].
2: The dominant soil types are moderately developed (Haploxerolls) with a loamy-sandy texture and superficial horizons containing moderate organic matter [ 47].
3: The typical vegetation consists mainly of a matrix dominated by the grasses  Pappostipa speciosa and  Festuca pallescens, and by scattered shrubs such as  Azorella prolifera and  Senecio bracteolatus.
4: Shrubs and grasses are intermixed with small native and exotic herbs [ 43].
