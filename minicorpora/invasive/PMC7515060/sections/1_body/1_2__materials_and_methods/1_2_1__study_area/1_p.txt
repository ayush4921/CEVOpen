0: Sri Lanka is an island country located in the Indian Ocean, between 554 and 952 north latitude and 7939 and 8153 east longitude, with an approximate area of 65,610 km 2.
1: Topographically, the country is divided into three peneplains [ 40].
2: The mountainous region located in the south-central part of Sri Lanka rising above 2500 m is surrounded by broad lowland plains [ 41].
3: The climate of Sri Lanka is generally tropical and warm; however, a wide variation can be observed across the country due to the differences in rainfall and elevation [ 41].
4: Sri Lanka is endowed with diverse vegetation types mainly due to variations in topography, climate, soil and also coastal influence [ 42].
5: The unique and diverse ecosystems have provided habitats for rich species diversity and genetic variability among species [ 41].
6: Out of the total land area of the country, nearly 35% is reserved for conservation purposes [ 40] and around 40% is utilized for agriculture [ 43].
