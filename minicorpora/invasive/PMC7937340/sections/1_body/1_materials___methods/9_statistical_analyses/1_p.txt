0: Average values and relative abundances of bacterial and fungal phyla and genera were calculated using Excel (Version 2010).
1: Significant differences in soil physiochemical parameters between the different plant species were determined using the Students  t-test in Excel.
2: Significant differences in the relative abundance of bacterial and fungal phyla and genera between the RSM of  A. adenophora and either of the two native plant species were determined using the KruskalWallis test with SPSS (version 16.0).
3: Significant differences were established at  P  0.05.
4: Venn figures and gene expression profiles were calculated and drawn using R software ( https://www.r-project.org).
