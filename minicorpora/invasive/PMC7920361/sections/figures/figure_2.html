Microstegium disease incidence. The percentage of Microstegium leaves per pot with lesions following B. gigantea or mock inoculation across the Microstegium density gradient in the presence of (A) Dichanthelium, (B) Elymus, and (C) Eragrostis. Observations (points and error bars, mean ± 95% confidence intervals) and model fits (lines and shaded ribbons, mean ± 95% credible intervals) are shown.
