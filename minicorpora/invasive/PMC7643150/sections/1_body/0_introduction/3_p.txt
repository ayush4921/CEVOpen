0: There are many types of models that can be used to evaluate ecological change.
1: Spatially explicit models include, among others, invasive species spread models, individual based models, network models, or potential distribution models.
2: There are also aspatial models such as vegetation dynamics models used to quantify vegetation class amounts within a landscape.
3: Area- or state- based models that inform how the condition or composition of a piece of land may change over time can provide key information to managers who are concerned with the future condition of a landscape.
