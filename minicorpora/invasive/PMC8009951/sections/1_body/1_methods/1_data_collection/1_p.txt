0: Data on 562 species of alien plants and their distribution in China were collected based on  The Checklist of the Chinese Invasive Plants35.
1: The alien plant species were distributed in 34 provincial-level administrative regionstwenty-three provinces, five autonomous regions, four municipalities, and two special administrative regions 2,  11,  35,  43,  44.
2: We collected the data on morphological traits of diaspores from several sources, including the Flora of China ( http://www.iplant.cn/foc/), the PLANTS database managed by the United States Department of Agriculture ( https://plants.usda.gov/java/), and the Seed Information Database from the Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew ( http://data.kew.org/sid/).
3: We retrieved references from the China National Knowledge Infrastructure database (CNKI,  https://www.cnki.net/), Baidu ( https://www.baidu.com/), and Web of Science ( http://apps.webofknowledge.com) using the Latin name of the alien plant species as our keywords, and reviewed them to obtain information related to dispersal modes.
