0: Common milkweed ( Asclepias syriaca L.) is an invasive perennial herb species of North American origin.
1: After recognizing the ongoing secondary range expansion, as well as the economic and ecological importance of common milkweed, it was included in the list of invasive alien species of European Union concern (EU Regulation 1143/2014).
2: It has already been naturalized in 23 countries worldwide, and anticipated climate changes are predicted to help further its future spread beyond its current distribution [ 20].
