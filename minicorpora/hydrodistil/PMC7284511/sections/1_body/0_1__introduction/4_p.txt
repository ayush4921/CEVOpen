0: Beilschmiedia erythrophloia Hayata is a tree found in Taiwan, southern China, Hainan Island, and Ryukyu Islands (Japan) [ 12, 13].
1: In Vietnam, the tree is found in Nghe An, Ha Tinh, and ong Nai provinces [ 14].
2: Previous phytochemical studies of  B. erythrophloia have revealed endiandric acid derivatives from the roots [ 15, 16], the cytotoxic alkaloid beischamide from the stems [ 13], and a leaf essential oil rich in ( E)-caryophyllene and -humulene [ 17].
