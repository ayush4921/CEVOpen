0: Rhododendron tomentosum plant shoots and inflorescences (up to 1.0 kg) were collected at various vegetative stages (from April to October, during several years) in the forest Samanis (Utena district, Eastern Lithuania) marshes.
1: The habitat of investigated population is depicted on the geographic information system map ( Figure S1).
2: A voucher specimen has been deposited in the Herbarium of the Institute of Botany (BILAS), number 68889.
3: Plant material was dried at room temperature (2025 C); leaves and inflorescences were separated before drying.
