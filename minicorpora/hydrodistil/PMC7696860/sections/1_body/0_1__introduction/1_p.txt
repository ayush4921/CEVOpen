0: Winter savory ( Satureja montana L.) represents an aromatic plant from the Lamiaceae family.
1: The benefits of  S. montana have long been recognized in traditional medicine which has established the plant as a widespread folk remedy.
2: Furthermore, winter savory is used as a spice.
3: S. montana is rich in essential oil with oxygen-containing phenolic monoterpenes carvacrol and thymol as its most dominant components [ 1].
4: In addition to lipophilic fraction, winter savory possesses significant bioactive components in its content which are hydrophilic in character, such as phenolic acids [ 2].
