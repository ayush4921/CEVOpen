0: Physical-chemical analyzes performed on  A. rosaeodora essential oil were: density, measured with a glass pycnometer; refractive index, calculated with an ABBE 2WAJ refractometer (PCE Instruments, Southampton, UK); the color and appearance, that were visually verified by three different people; and the determination of solubility, carried out through the ratio of 1:1 of oil and 70% ethanol until its complete solubilization.
