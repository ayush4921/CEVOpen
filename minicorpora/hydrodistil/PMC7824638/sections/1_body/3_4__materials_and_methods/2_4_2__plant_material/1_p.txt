0: Authentic samples of the  A. rosaeodora species were obtained from three trees cultivated at the Adolpho Ducke Forest Reserve, Highway AM-010, km 26 (latitude 2.908185, longitude 59.975457), Manaus, Brazil.
1: Leaves and thin branches were harvested with a trimmer from the treetops in the dry season, March 2017.
2: The taxonomic identification was undertaken by the Herbarium of the Department of Botany of the Universidade Federal do Amazonas, registry number 5982.
3: The leaves were selected and dried in an oven at 37 C for 48 h and sprayed in an electric knife mill at the Food and Water Quality Control Laboratory of the Federal University of Maranhao.
