0: The microbial strains used in this study were obtained from the Faculty of Pharmacy, Mashhad University of Medical Sciences, Iran.
1: These strains included one Gram-positive bacterium,  Staphylococcus aureus (ATCC 25923), one Gram-negative strain,  Pseudomonas aeruginosa (PTCC1707), and one mold including  Aspergillus niger (PTCC 5010) and one yeast  Candida albicans (PTCC 5027).
2: Bacterial strains were cultured at 37 C for 24 h at Muller hinton agar (Merck, Germany) medium, and fungai were cultured at Potato dextrose agar (Merck, Germany) for 72 h at 25 C.
