0: MAHD was performed at less than 100 C and atmospheric pressure by a microwave equipment (MicRosynth, Milestone, USA).
1: 200 g of grinded coriander seeds was poured into a 2000 ml flask and added to a 1500 ml of distilled water, then placed in a microwave oven.
2: A condenser was placed above (outside the oven) to collect the obtained essential oil.
3: First for 10 min at 800 W power reaching less than 100 C and then after the boiling temperature was extracted for 60 min with 500 W power.
4: The final 10 min was considered for ventilation ( Kosar et al., 2005).
