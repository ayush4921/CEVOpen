0: The leaves of  A. repens were collected from Neyriz, east of Fars Province (Iran), in April 2019.
1: The air-dried and grounded leaves of  A. repens were extracted using 70% aqueous solution of ethanol, methanol, and acetone.
2: Then 10 g of dry powder of leaves was separately subjected to solvent extraction using 100 ml of solvents for one day.
3: The mixture was then filtered on Whatman No.
4: 1 filter paper, and a second extraction was performed.
5: Both supernatants were mixed and concentrated in vacuum at 40 C. The extraction for each solvent was conducted three times, and the obtained extracts were stored at 4 C until further analysis.
