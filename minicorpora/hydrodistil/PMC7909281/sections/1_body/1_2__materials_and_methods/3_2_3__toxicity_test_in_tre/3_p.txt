0: After EO application, samples were mixed by a vortex for 1 min to homogenize the treatment.
1: The control treatment consisted of five Falcons with 8 g of wheat treated with 1 mL of acetone only.
2: Treated wheat dried for 15 min under hood to eliminate the acetone.
3: Then, twenty insects per falcon were added to the wheat and Falcon were closed by a tulle to allow air circulation.
4: Tubes were placed under controlled condition (28 C  1 C; 75  5% RH).
5: Temperature and humidity were chosen as the optimum for  S. granarius [ 36] and to be representative of the conditions at the harvest period.
6: Five repetitions were performed for each concentration.
