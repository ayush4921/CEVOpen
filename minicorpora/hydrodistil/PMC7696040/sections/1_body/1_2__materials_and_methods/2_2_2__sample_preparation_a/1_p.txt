0: Essential oils were produced using hydrodistillation.
1: Approximately 600 g of fresh leaf was removed from the twig then cut into 0.5 mm fragments and placed into a 5 L round bottom flask with 2.5 L of deionised distilled water (ddH 2O).
2: Leaves were heated for 34 h using continuous cohobation of hydrosol.
3: Afterwards the steam/oil mix was collected from the 1000 mL separating funnel after separating from the hydrosol.
4: Essential oils were stored away from light at 4 C until used.
