0: Approximately 100 g of each sample (PRM, PRF, SPM, SPF, CoPR, and CoSP) were submitted to a hydrodistillation process which was performed for 2 h by using a Clevenger-type apparatus.
1: The essential oils were collected in ethyl ether and the organic layers were dried employing anhydrous Na 2SO 4.
2: Then, the obtained solutions were filtered and the solvents were removed under reduced pressure to obtain the essential oils named PRMV and PRFV from Parana state and male and female specimens, respectively.
3: Samples from Sao Paulo state were named SPMV and SPFV for male and female specimens, respectively, and commercial samples from PR and SP states were named PRCoV and SPCoV, respectively
