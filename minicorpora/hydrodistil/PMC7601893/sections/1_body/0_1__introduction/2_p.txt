0: Several studies have been conducted in order to verify the chemical composition and pharmacological effects of  Baccharis genus extracts and fractions [ 6, 8, 9].
1: Phytochemical analyses have indicated the presence of a wide range of bioactive compounds, mainly polyphenols such as isoquercetin, apigenin, quercetin, rutin, and hispidulin [ 10].
2: These compounds are well-known as a consequence of their free-radical scavenging properties and for inhibiting lipid peroxidation [ 11].
