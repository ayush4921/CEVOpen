0: The  Origanum genus (Lamiaceae family) includes approximately 38 species that have been studied extensively for potential importance, and uses in flavoring foods and traditional medicine due to their pharmacological characteristics 1.
1: Origanum species are widely distributed in North Africa, eastern Mediterranean, and Siberian regions.
2: According to Kokkini 2,  Origanum taxa are rich in essential oils that exhibit well-known antioxidant and antimicrobial properties.
3: Based on the chemical composition of essential oils,  Origanum species have been classified into three main chemotypes: thymol/carvacrol, linalool/terpinen-4-ol, and sesquiterpenes.
