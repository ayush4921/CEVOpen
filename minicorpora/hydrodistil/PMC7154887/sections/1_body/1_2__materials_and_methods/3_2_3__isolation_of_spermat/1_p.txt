0: Male laboratory mice (3-6 days old) were purchased from the National Medical Research Institute (NMRI) and maintained in accordance with the guidelines issued by Tehran University of Medical Sciences.
1: Spermatogonial stem cells were isolated in two steps of enzymatic digestion from testis tissue.
2: Phosphate buffered saline (PBS; Sigma, Munich, Germany) was applied to wash the collected testes of mice.
3: Testes were minced into small pieces after removing the tunica albuginea and then digested in a medium containing collagenase type IV (1 mg/mL; Sigma), hyaluronidase (0.5 mg/mL; Sigma), and DNase (10 g/mL; Sigma) in minimum essential medium alpha (MEM; Sigma) for 20 min.
4: The tubules were separated with shaking at 37 C in 5% CO 2.
5: The cells were centrifuged at 1500  g for 5 min and then washed twice with PBS [ 28].
