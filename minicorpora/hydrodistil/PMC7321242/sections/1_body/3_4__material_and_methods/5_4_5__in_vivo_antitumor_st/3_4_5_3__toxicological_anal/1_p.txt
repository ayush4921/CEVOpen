0: The animals were weighed at the beginning and the end of the experiment to assess the toxicological effects.
1: All animals were observed for signs of abnormality throughout the study.
2: Hematological analyses were performed using an Advia 60 hematology system (Bayer, Leverkusen, Germany).
3: Livers, kidneys, lungs and hearts were collected, weighed and examined for signs of thick lesion formation, color change and/or hemorrhage.
4: After macroscopic examination, the tumors, livers, kidneys, lungs and hearts were fixed in 4% buffered formalin and embedded in paraffin.
5: The tissue sections were stained with hematoxylin and eosin (and Periodic acid-Schiff for kidneys slides), and a pathologist performed the analysis under an optical microscope.
