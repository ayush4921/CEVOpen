0: Fifty C.B-17 severe combined immunodeficient (SCID) mice (females, 2530 g) were obtained and kept at the animal facilities of the Goncalo Moniz Institute-FIOCRUZ (Salvador, Bahia, Brazil).
1: The animals were housed in cages with free access to food and water.
2: All animals were subjected to a 12:12 h lightdark cycle (lights on at 6:00 a.m.).
3: The animal ethics committee of the Goncalo Moniz Institute-FIOCRUZ approved the experimental protocol used (number #06/2015).
