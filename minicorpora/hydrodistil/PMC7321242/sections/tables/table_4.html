Beginning 1 day after tumor implantation, the animals were treated intraperitoneally for 21 consecutive days. The negative control (CTL) was treated with a vehicle (5% DMSO) used to dilute EO. 5-Fluorouracil (5-FU, 10 mg/kg) was used as a positive control. The data are presented as the mean ± S.E.M. of 10–20 animals.
