0: Edible films are thin layers that are used on the foodstuffs and play a key role in the distribution, marketing, and protection of food products against mechanical damage and microbial and chemical activity.
1: These films are biodegradable, nontoxic, and environmentally friendly (Bourtoom,  2008).
2: Owing to the nonbiodegradable of synthetic polymers, the use of biodegradable materials in the food packaging industry has been considered (Ghanbarzadeh et al.,  2007).
3: The use of composite films is one of the most widely used methods to improve the properties of the films produced from one component (Cao, Fu, & He,  2007; Yoo & Krochta,  2011).
