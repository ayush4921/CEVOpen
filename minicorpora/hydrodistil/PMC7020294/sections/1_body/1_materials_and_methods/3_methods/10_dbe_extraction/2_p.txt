0: The hydrodistillation method by the Clevenger apparatus was used to extract DbE.
1: About 200 g  Dianthus barbatus was ground and then was poured in Clevenger apparatus flasks.
2: After adding distilled water to twothirds of flasks, they were attached to the Clevenger device, its contents were heated for 4 hr, and then, essential oil was separated and collected from distilled water.
