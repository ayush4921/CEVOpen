0: The antimicrobial activity of extracts and essential oils of fruits and leaves of  P. dioica has been demonstrated.
1: P. dioica leaf extracts showed significant antimicrobial properties against  Staphylococcus aureus,  Streptococcus mutans,  Escherichia coli,  Bacillus cereus,  Salmonella typhimurium,  Candida albicans,  Pseudomonas fluorescens,  Bacillus megaterium,  Aspergillus niger, and  Penicillium sp.
2: [ 17, 18, 19, 20].
3: In another study the essential oil of  P. dioica leaves showed growth inhibition of  Staphylococcus aureus,  Escherichia coli,  Salmonella typhi,  Pseudomonas aeruginosa, and  Bacillus cereus [ 21].
4: The essential oil of  P. dioica fruits showed significant antimicrobial properties against  Acinetobacter baumannii,  Staphylococcus aureus,  Pseudomonas aeruginosa,  Candida albicans, and other yeasts of the  Candida genus [ 8, 22].
