0: The leaves and fruits of  P. dioica were collected from six trees selected at random with the aid of Qeqchi people in April 2014 in the community of Barra Lampara, Livingston, Izabal Department at an altitude of 13 meters above sea level (masl).
1: A voucher sample was deposited in the Herbarium of the School of Biology (BIGU) of the University of San Carlos with code BIGU 72838.
2: Collected plant material was separated into leaves and fruits, the they were dried in a soler dryer at temperature between 28 and 32 C. Leaves and fruits were milled and extracted separately.
