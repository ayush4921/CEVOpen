0: Control experiments were performed with 1% dimethyl sulphoxide (DMSO) diluted with distilled and sterile water.
1: Each essential oil (ET01, ET02 and ET03) was dissolved in DMSO to obtain different solutions in the following concentrations: 1 g/ml, 200 mg/ml, 40 mg/ml, 10 mg/ml, 2 mg/l, 0.2 mg/ml and 0.02 mg/ml.
2: Then, each stock solution was diluted in natural mineral water to prepare the different test solutions.
