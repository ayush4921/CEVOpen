0: Miracidia from NMRI strain were hatched from eggs recovered from hamster livers after 50 days post-infection.
1: Eggs were filtered and washed to obtain miracidia which were collected under illumination.
2: About 300 freshly hatched miracidia were used to test the toxicity of the essential oil from  E. triquetrum.
3: Swimming behavior and mortality were investigated after 1 and 4 h of treatments.
4: All observations were done using Zeiss LSM microscope and stereoscopic microscope.
