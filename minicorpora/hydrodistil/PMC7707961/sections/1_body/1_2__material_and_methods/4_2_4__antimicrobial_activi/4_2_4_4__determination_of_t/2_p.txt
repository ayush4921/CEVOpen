0: (1) RPMI1640 Preparation.
1: RPMI powder (1.04 g) was dissolved into 90 mL of sterile distilled water.
2: Afterward, MOP (3.456 g) was added to the solution.
3: To adjust the pH of the solution to 7, a 1 M NaOH aqueous solution was added.
4: Finally, to reach a volume of 100 mL, distilled and sterilized water was added to make up the difference.
5: Using a 0.45  m syringe filter, the solution was sterilized at room temperature.
