0: The dried seeds of  Myristica fragrans (nutmeg) were from Grenada.
1: The seeds were identified by Jurga Bernatoniene, Medical Academy, Lithuania University of Health Sciences, Kaunas, Lithuania.
2: A voucher specimen (I 18922) was placed for storage at the Herbarium of the Department of Drug Technology and Social Pharmacy, Lithuanian University of Health Sciences, Lithuania.
3: The seeds were a brown-beige color, had a characteristic odor, and strong, bitter, and spicy flavor.
4: Dried nutmeg seeds were ground into a powder (particles smaller than 0.5 mm) with a laboratory mill.
5: The samples were kept in an airtight container in a dark environment and in ambient temperature.
