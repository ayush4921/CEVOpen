0: Samples were prepared from nutmeg seeds and purified with water.
1: The solid material was 15 g and the water was 300 mL.
2: A Clevenger-type apparatus (European pharmacopoeia) for hydrodistillation was used.
3: Hydrodistillation was carried out for 4 h. A colorless essential oil with specific odor was obtained.
4: The essential oil was collected with water in airtight bottle and stored in a refrigerator at 4 C until needed.
