0: Extracts were also prepared by using ultrasound-assisted extraction.
1: This method was used as a simple and effective method for nutmeg extracts.
2: The 1 g material and 20 mL solvent were used.
3: We used different concentrations of ethanol for extraction (96%, 70%, and 50%).
4: The flask with substance was put in an ultrasound bath (Sonorex Digitec DT 156 BH, Germany).
5: Extracts were prepared on 25 C temperature for 30 min.
6: After that, the extracts were filtered with paper filter and stored in a refrigerator at 4 C until further analysis.
