0: Fresh  C. osmophloeum leaves were collected from Lienhuachih Research Center of Taiwan Forestry Research Institute in Nantou County, Taiwan.
1: Leaves were hydrodistilled in a Clevenger apparatus for 6 h to obtain the leaf essential oil.
2: The yield of leaf essential oil was 3.46  0.06% ( w/w).
3: Leaf essential oil was stored in the dark glass bottle and kept in the refrigerator at 4 C.
