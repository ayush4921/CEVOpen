0: The GC-MS system (Agilent, Little Falls, DE, USA) was composed on a 5975 mass selective detector and a 6890 GC.
1: The GC capillary column was an Agilent HP-5MS (30 m  0.25 mm  0.25 m).
2: The injector and detector temperatures were 220 and 260 C, respectively.
3: The oven temperature was held at 45 C for 5 min, increased to 250 C with a flow rate of 3 C /min, and then held for 10 min.
4: A 1 L aliquot of sample was injected in the split mode with a ratio of 1:10.
5: Helium was used as the carrier gas with a flow rate of 1 mL/min.
