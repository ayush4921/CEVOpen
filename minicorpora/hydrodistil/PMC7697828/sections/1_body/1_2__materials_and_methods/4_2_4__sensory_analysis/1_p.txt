0: Sensory evaluation was carried out in the sensory laboratory of the Department of Vegetables and Medicinal Plants, WULS-SGGW.
1: Quantitative descriptive analysis (QDA) was used.
2: The evaluation was determined on the fresh herb of both subspecies, collected in the first cut (at the beginning of blooming).
3: Attributes of its taste and odor were selected and estimated.
4: In order to select attributes, brainstorming sessions were done by an expert panel consisting of a minimum of 10 assessors.
5: Evaluation was performed in two independent sessions.
