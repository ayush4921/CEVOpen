0: The fruit of  L. formosana (Chinese name LuLuTong) has been used as a traditional Chinese medicine for thousands of years [ 3]; the pharmacology and phytochemistry of  L. formosana has been reviewed [ 4].
1: The fruit extract of  L. formosana has shown anti-inflammatory activity [ 5].
2: The fruits of  L. formosana have yielded lupane and oleanane triterpenoids [ 6].
3: The fruit essential oil has shown antifungal activity and was dominated by -pinene (16.8%), ( E)-caryophyllene (10.1%), -muurolol (8.3%), -cadinol (7.6%), -pinene (6.7%), and sabinene (5.7%) [ 7].
