0: Essential oil from the fruits of  E. umbellata were extracted through hydro distillation method using a Clevenger type apparatus connected with a condenser [ 19].
1: Distillation process was continued for 3 days at 100 C, and the fruit volatile oils, yellowish in color were collected in glass bottles.
2: Anhydrous sodium sulfate was used to remove water from extracted oil.
3: Finally the oil was properly sealed in glass vials and stored at  30 C till further analysis/use in refrigerator (HF3-700S, USA).
