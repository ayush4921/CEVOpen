0: Other diseases that have attracted attention due to their impact on public health are those caused by protozoa, such as Chagas disease, caused by the protozoan  Trypanosoma cruzi.
1: T. cruzi is a parasite with continental dispersion present in 21 countries in Latin America.
2: There is a worldwide estimate of around 6 to 7 million infections and approximately 75 million living in endemic areas at risk of infection [ 9].
3: Nifurtimox and benznidazole are the only treatments used in patients with Chagas disease, causing about 40% of side effects in adult patients, making treatment adherence difficult [ 9 11].
4: There are still cases of strains resistant to these treatments [ 12].
