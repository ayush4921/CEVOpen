0: Fifty grams dried leaves of  B.  balsamifera were distilled to collect 5 L of distillate (distillation time: 10 h), and the distillate was collected for extracting total volatiles (TV) of the leaves using same volume anhydrous diethyl ether under room temperature.
1: The extract solution was dehydrated with anhydrous sodium sulfate and concentrated to obtain total volatiles.
2: The TV was stored at 20C in dark glass bottles until required.
3: The process was executed in ten replicates.
