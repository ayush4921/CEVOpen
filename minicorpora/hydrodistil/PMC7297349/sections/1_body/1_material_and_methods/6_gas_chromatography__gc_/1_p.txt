0: The oil samples for GC analysis were prepared in 99%  n-hexane.
1: The final concentration was 0.5 mg/mL.
2: GC analysis was executed on a Shimadzu GC-2010 gas chromatograph operated with a split/splitless injector and a Shimadzu AOC-20i autoinjector (Shimadzu, Kyoto, Japan).
3: Column: CP-WAX capillary column (30 m  0.25 mm  25 m) (Varian, USA) and DB-5 capillary column (30 m  0.25 mm  25 m) (Agilent, USA).
4: Temperature program: from 45C (1 min) to 280C (3 min) at 5C/min.
5: Injection temperature: 250C.
6: Injection volume: 1.0 L. Carrier gas: Nitrogen, flow rate: 3 mL/min.
7: Injection mode: split (1:5).
8: FID temperature: 250C.
9: H 2 flow: 47 mL/min; air flow: 400 mL/min.
10: Data handling was carried out by means of GCsolution 2.3 (Shimadzu).
