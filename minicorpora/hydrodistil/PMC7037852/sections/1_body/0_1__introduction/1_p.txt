0: Myristica fragrans Houtt., (Myristicaceae), a plant species indigenous to Indonesia, is widely distributed in the humid tropical and coastal regions, and is known as nutmeg, for being used in the production of the spices nutmeg and mace [ 1, 2, 3].
1: Historically, nutmeg was believed to be imported to Europe by Arab traders during the 12th century [ 4], where grated nutmeg was used as a sachet, and the Romans used it as incense.
2: In the 1600s nutmeg became a pricy commercial spice in the Western world [ 5].
