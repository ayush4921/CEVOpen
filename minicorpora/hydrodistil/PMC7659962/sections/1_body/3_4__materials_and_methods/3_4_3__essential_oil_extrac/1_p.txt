0: Essential oils were obtained by hydrodistillation of dried plant material using a Clevenger-type apparatus, as previously described [ 59].
1: We used conditions accepted by the European Pharmacopoeia (European Directorate for the Quality of Medicines, Council of Europe, Strasbourg, France, 2014) to avoid artifacts.

3: Stock solutions of the essential oils were prepared in DMSO (10 mg/mL) for biological evaluation.
