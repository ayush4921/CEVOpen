0: Three-dimensional conformers of carvacrol, thymol, -myrcene, and  p-cymene were downloaded from the PubChem database (compound ID numbers 10364, 6989, 31253, and 7463, respectively) and imported into MVD.
1: Docking was performed with a rigid receptor and flexible ligands.
2: For each compound, 300 docking runs were performed.
3: For the docking poses obtained, additional geometry optimizations were carried out using the Energy Minimization and Optimize HBonds options of the MVD program.
