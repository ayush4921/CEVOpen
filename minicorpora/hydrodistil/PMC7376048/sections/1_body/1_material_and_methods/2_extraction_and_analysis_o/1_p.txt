0: The extraction method was hydrodistillation in Clevenger apparatus, for 4 h, according to the methodology recommended by Farmacopeia Brasileira for volatile oils 28.
1: About 200 g of fresh leaves were used in approximately 1 L of reverse osmosis water in a 2-L round-bottom flask.
2: The flask was then coupled to the apparatus, where the oil was extracted for three hours after water boiling.
3: The obtained hydrolate was centrifuged at 5,000 rpm for 3 min to promote the separation between the aqueous and oily phases.
4: With the aid of a Pasteur pipette, the (supernatant) oil was removed and stored in an amber bottle in a freezer at  20 C, protected from light 4,  29.
