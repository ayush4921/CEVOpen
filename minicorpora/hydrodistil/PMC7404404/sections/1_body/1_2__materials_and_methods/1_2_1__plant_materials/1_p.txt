0: The leaves of  L. taraxacifolia were collected from Ipara, Badagry (6454.07 N and 25252.75 E) Lagos state, Nigeria.
1: Botanical identification was done at the Herbarium, University of Lagos, Nigeria, where a voucher specimen (LUH: 7959) was deposited.
2: Fresh leaves of  L. taraxacifolia were cut into pieces, air dried, and pulverized in a blender to increase the surface area.
3: A 450-g sample of blended  L. taraxacifolia was hydrodistilled for 4 h in an all-glass modified Clevenger-type apparatus according to British Pharmacopoeia [ 35].
4: The obtained essential oil was stored in a sealed glass bottle with a screw lid cover under refrigeration at 4 C until ready for use.
5: Oil yield was calculated on a dry weight basis.
