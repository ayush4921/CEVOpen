0: The dried seeds of nutmeg ( Myristica fragrans) were from Grenada.
1: Seeds were identified by Jurga Bernatoniene, Medical Academy, Lithuania University of Health Sciences, Kaunas, Lithuania.
2: A voucher specimen (I 18922) was placed for storage at the Herbarium of the Department of Drug Technology and Social Pharmacy.
3: The seeds had a characteristic odor, a strong, bitter, and spicy flavour, and they were a brown-beige color.
4: The seeds were ground into a powder (using laboratory mill), with particles smaller than 0.5 mm.
5: All powder samples were kept in a dark and airtight container at 20  2 C.
