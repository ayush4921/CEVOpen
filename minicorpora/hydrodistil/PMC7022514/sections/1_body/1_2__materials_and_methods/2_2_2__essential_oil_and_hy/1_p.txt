0: The essential oil from nutmeg seeds was prepared by using hydrodistillation.
1: The modified Clevenger type apparatus was used.
2: Two samples of essential oil were prepared: one without excipient and the other with 1% of magnesium aluminometasilicate.
3: Each sample was prepared with 15 g of nutmeg powder and 300 mL distilled water, and 1% magnesium aluminometasilicate was used as an excipient in one of the samples.
4: Also, hydrolat of these two essential oils was used.
5: It was collected from Clevenger apparatus.
6: This material was collected first, followed by the essential oil.
7: All samples were obtained and stored in airtight bottles in the refrigerator.
8: The hydrodistillation took 4 h.
