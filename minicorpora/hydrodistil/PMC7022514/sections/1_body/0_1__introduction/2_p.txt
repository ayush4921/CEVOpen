0: Nutmeg oil preparations are also known for their antioxidant capacity.
1: Using the 2,2-diphenyl-1-picrylhydrazyl (DPPH) free radical scavenging assay, Piaru et al.
2: reported a significant antioxidant activity of nutmeg oil [ 15].
3: The antioxidant properties are often related to the alleviation of inflammation.
4: Nutmeg oil diminished chronic inflammation and pain through the inhibition of COX-2 expression and substance P release in vivo [ 16].
5: In another study, nutmeg oil suppressed reactive oxygen species (ROS) production in human neutrophils stimulated by PMA (phorbol 12-myristate 13-acetate) [ 17] and mildly inhibited phagocytosis in human neutrophils [ 18].
6: However, there is no published research on the effect of nutmeg seed essential oil on virus-triggered inflammatory response.
