0: Teucrium species have been shown a rich source of  neo-clerodane diterpenoids that, among the non-volatile constituents, are considered as chemotaxonomic markers of the genus.
1: Up to now, 279  neo-clerodanes have been identified and their occurrence has been largely reviewed [ 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, 15].
2: Other metabolites isolated from species of this genus include abietane diterpenes, sesquiterpenes, triterpenes, steroids, flavonoids, iridoids and aromatic compounds [ 15].
