0: Scabies is a contagious skin disease caused by the infestation of  Sarcoptes scabiei mites.
1: The clinical symptoms include intense itch, erythema, and more severe complications due to secondary bacterial infections [ 1].
2: Scabies was added to the WHO list of neglected tropical diseases in 2017, which highlights the substantial global health and economic burden, especially in low income populations [ 2, 3].
