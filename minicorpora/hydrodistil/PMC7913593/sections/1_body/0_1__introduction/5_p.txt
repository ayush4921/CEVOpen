0: Based on a literature survey on dedicated databases (Scopus, ScienceDirect, SpringerLink, PubMed, Web of Science), the aim of this review paper is to be a critical discussion of aspects regarding classical extraction versus modern extraction techniques; possibilities to scale up (advantages and disadvantages of different methods usually used and the influence of extraction parameters); and different medicinal and aromatic plants different applications (medical and industrial applications, as well as the potential use in nanotechnology).
1: In addition, future perspectives are critically discussed.
