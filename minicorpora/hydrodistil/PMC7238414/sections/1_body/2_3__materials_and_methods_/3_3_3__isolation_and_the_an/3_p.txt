0: The GC-FID-MS analyses were performed using the Trace GC Ultra apparatus (Thermo Electron Corporation, Waltham, MA, USA) with FID and MS DSQ II detectors and the FID-MS splitter (SGE).
1: Operating conditions: apolar capillary column Rtx-1ms (Restek), 60 m  0.25 mm i.d., film thickness 0.25 m; temperature program, 50300 C at 4 C/min; SSL injector temperature 280 C; FID temperature 300 C; split ratio 1:20; carrier gas helium at a regular pressure 200 kPa.
2: Mass spectra were acquired over the mass range 30400 Da, ionization voltage 70 eV; ion source temperature 200 C.
