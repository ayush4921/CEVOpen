0: Leaves from  L. nobilis L. were collected only from wild growing trees, from one location per country.
1: Subsamples were generated from 10 different trees per location by sampling leaves at different height and exposure within a tree.
2: The leaves were harvested at the end of vegetation, during the formation of fruits.
3: The leaves were collected in October 2016 from the Athos peninsula on a land belonging to the Bulgarian monastery (North Greece, at 160 m elev., 400926N and 241935E) and in December 2016 from the province of Meria (West Georgia, at 200 m elev., 41 56 27 N 41 53 45 E).
