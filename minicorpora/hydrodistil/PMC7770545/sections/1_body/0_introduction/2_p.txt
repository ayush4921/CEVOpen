0: Laurel ( Laurus nobilis L.) fam.
1: Lauraceae is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region and cultivated in a number of countries in Asia, Europe, and the Americas as a spice, or used as ornamental plant ( Parthasarathy et al., 2008).
2: Laurel leaves, also known as bay leaves, are a major spice, which has been used traditionally as an ingredient for improving food flavor and taste.
3: Naturally occurring biologically active compounds in leaves include terpenes, terpene derivates, polyphenols, alkaloids, minerals, vitamins ( Caputo et al., 2017;  Chahal et al., 2017;  Parthasarathy et al., 2008).
