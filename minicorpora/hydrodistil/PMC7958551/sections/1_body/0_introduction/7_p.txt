0: Lysozyme, known as Nacetylmuramide glycanhydrolase comprised of 129 amino acids, is an animalderived antimicrobial single peptide enzyme and considered GRAS by FDA (Chen et al.,  2020; Syngai & Ahmed,  2019).
1: It is documented that lysozyme has good antimicrobial activity, especially against grampositive bacteria.
2: As it is a relatively heatstable enzyme, it can be applied to extend shelf life and to enhance the safety of a variety of foods (Aziz & Karboune,  2018; Janek et al.,  2020; Pellegrini et al.,  1992).
