0: There are little, if any, published data on the use of  Ziziphora clinopodioides essential oil (ZCEO) and lysozyme for enhancing the safety and shelf life of fresh sausages.
1: This study was done in order to investigate (i) the effects of ZCEO and lysozyme for the control of  L. monocytogenes inoculated (3 log CFU/g) in fresh sausage during a 13day storage period and (ii) the possibility of shelf life extension of fresh sausage using the above combination.
