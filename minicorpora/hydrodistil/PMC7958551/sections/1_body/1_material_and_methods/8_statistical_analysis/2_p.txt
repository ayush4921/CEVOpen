0: The recorded data were analyzed using SPSS software (version 16.0 for Windows), and statistical significance (significance level was considered  p < .05) among parameters in each day was evaluated by oneway analysis of variance (ANOVA) and followed by Tukey test.
1: Trends of changes in each treatment in different days were analyzed statistically using repeated measure test.
2: After that, results were declared as mean value with their standard deviation (mean   SD).
3: The normality of the data was evaluated by onesample KolmogorovSmirnov test.
4: All measurements were performed in triplicate (Khanjari et al.,  2019).
