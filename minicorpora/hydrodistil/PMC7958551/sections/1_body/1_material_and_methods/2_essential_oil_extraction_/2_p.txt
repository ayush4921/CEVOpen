0: The fresh leaves of  Ziziphora clinopodioides were gathered at the full flowering stage in MayJuly 2018 from natural growing plants located in Kermanshah Province Mountains, Iran.
1: Then, ground airdried leaves (100 g) of  Z. clinopodioides and distilled water (2000 ml) were subjected to a Clevenger apparatus system and steam distillated for 3 hr.
2: Next, the collected essential oil was kept in an opaque glass tube under refrigerated condition until further use.
3: The components of the used essential oil were identified by gas chromatographymass spectrometer in our previous study (Hasan et al.,  2019).
