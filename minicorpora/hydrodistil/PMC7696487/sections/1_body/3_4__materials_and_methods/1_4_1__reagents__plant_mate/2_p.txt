0: Cubeba pepper fruits ( Figure 4) were purchased commercially from the Saudi market (Baljurashi city, Albaha, Kingdom Saudi Arabia).
1: Samples were cleaned well with tap water, sterilized, and dried in an oven at 60 C for 72 h before being pulverized into a fine powder for possible use.
2: Dried powder of the fruits (800 g) was subjected to hydrodistillation for 4 h using a Clevenger-type apparatus [ 51].
3: The resulting essential oils were dried using anhydrous sodium sulfate, and stored in sealed vials in the dark, at 4 C for subsequent analysis.
