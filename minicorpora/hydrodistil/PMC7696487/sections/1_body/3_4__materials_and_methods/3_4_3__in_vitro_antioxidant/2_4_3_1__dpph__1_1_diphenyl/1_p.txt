0: The DPPH test was carried out as described by Felhi et al.
1: [ 56].
2: A series of dilutions was prepared from the  P. cubeba fruits essential oil solution, each one supplemented with 1 mL of the ethanolic solution of DPPH.
3: Then, the dilutions were incubated for 30 min in the dark, and the absorbance of the mixtures was measured at 517 nm against a negative control treated under the same conditions.
4: Ascorbic acid (AA) was used in this test as the positive control.
