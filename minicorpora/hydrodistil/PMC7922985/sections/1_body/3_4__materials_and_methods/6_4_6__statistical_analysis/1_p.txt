0: Means, standard deviations (SD), min and max values were used for descriptive statistics of results.
1: The Spearman rank correlation analysis was used to test correlation of percentage of -terpinyl acetate with slope size.
2: The MannWhitney U test was used to estimate the differences between effects of essential oil and control (and between analytical standard and control) on germination parameters (GP, MGD, GI) of  P. pratensis,  P. pratense,  T. pratense,  H. perforatum, and  T. pulegioides.
3: Students  t-test was used to estimate the differences between effects of essential oil and control (and between analytical standard and control) on radicles development of the same plants.
4: Statistical data processing was carried out with the STATISTICA 7 and MS Excel software.
