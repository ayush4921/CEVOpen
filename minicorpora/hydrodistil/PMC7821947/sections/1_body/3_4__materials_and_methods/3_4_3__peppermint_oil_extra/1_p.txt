0: The leaves of  Mentha piperita Linn.
1: was purchased from Dong-Yang Herb Inc. (Seoul, Korea).
2: The essential oil extraction was proceeded by hydrodistillation method.
3: Briefly, 100 g of  M. piperita Linn.
4: was placed within a 1 L round-bottom flask with clevenger apparatus.
5: Steam distillation was performed for 6 h at 100 C and the obtained volatile oil was 1.6 mL (yield of MEO: 1.60%).
6: A voucher specimen (01-02-01-KR-191217) was deposited at Department of Convergence Korean Medical Science, College of Korean Medicine, Kyung Hee University, Seoul, Korea.
