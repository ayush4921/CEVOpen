0: Eggs of  Aedes aegypti were purchased from Institute of Biotechnology, Vietnam Academy of Science and Technology and maintained at the Laboratory of Department of Pharmacy of Duy Tan University, Da Nang, Vietnam.
1: Adults of  Culex quinquefasciatus and  Aedes albopictus collected in Hoa Khanh Nam ward, Lien Chieu district, Da Nang city (160314.9 N, 1080931.2 E) and were identified by National institute of Malariology, Parasitology, and Entomology, Ho Chi Minh City.
2: Adult mosquitoes were maintained in entomological cages (40  40  40 cm) and fed a 10% sucrose solution and were allowed to blood feed on 1-week-old chicks and mice, respectively.
3: Egg hatchings were induced with tap water.
4: Larvae were reared in plastic trays (24  35  5 cm).
5: The larvae were fed on Koi fish food.
6: All developmental stages were maintained at 25  2 C, 6575% relative humidity and a 12:12 h light:dark cycle at the Laboratory of the Faculty of Environmental and Chemical Engineering of Duy Tan University, Da Nang, Vietnam.
