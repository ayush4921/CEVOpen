0: Fresh  S. rosmarinus areal parts (0.74 and 1.20 kg for R1 and R2, respectively) were subject to hydrodistillation for 3 h using a Clevenger-type apparatus [ 57].
1: The white-yellow, obtained essential oils (3.21 and 7.01 mL) were dried over anhydrous sodium sulphate, stored in hermetically sealed brown glass bottles, and kept at 4 C before analysis.
2: Yields (w/w) of 0.58 and 0.41% for R1 and R2, respectively, were obtained.
