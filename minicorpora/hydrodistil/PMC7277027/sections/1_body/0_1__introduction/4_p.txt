0: Solvent-free microwave extraction is a new technique which combines microwave heating with dry distillation at atmospheric pressure for the isolation and concentration of the essential oils in plant materials.
1: In the solvent-free microwave extraction method, there is no need to add any solvent or water if fresh plant material is used.
2: For dry plant material, the sample is rehydrated by soaking in water for some time, followed by draining off the excess water [ 25].
