0: Aqueous solutions containing 1% (w/v) dry matter of aromatic plants were prepared in glass bottles and autoclaved for 20 min at 121 C. Aliquots of each plant extract were filtered through a 0.22 m nylon filter before injected to the HPLC system.
1: HPLC analysis was performed utilizing an Agilent 1200 system (Agilent Technology, Urdorf, Switzerland) equipped with a quaternary pump and a rheodyne injector valve with a 20 L loop.
