0: Fifty grams of each dried comminute plant material from oregano, thyme and Satureja was submitted, separately, to a hydrodistillation process (450 mL of deionized water) in a Clevenger-type apparatus for 2 h. The essential oil was collected, then dried over anhydrous sodium sulphate (Na 2SO 4) and finally stored at 4 C in the dark for further use.
