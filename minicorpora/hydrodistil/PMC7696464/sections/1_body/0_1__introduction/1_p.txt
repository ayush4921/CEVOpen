0: Generally recognized as safe (GRAS), aromatic plants are excellent alternatives for chemical additives used in the food industry.
1: Plants of the  Lamiaceae family, mainly found in the Mediterranean region, are utilized to produce essential oils that show antioxidant and antimicrobial properties [ 1].
2: Consumer demand for less processed foods, together with the increased concern about the use of synthetic preservatives, have directed the interest of the food industry towards natural sources of preservatives.
3: Essential oils have been used in vitro for their antimicrobial and antioxidant properties in different fungi and bacterial species, aiming to use them as a promising technology in order to increase the shelf life of foods [ 2, 3, 4, 5].
