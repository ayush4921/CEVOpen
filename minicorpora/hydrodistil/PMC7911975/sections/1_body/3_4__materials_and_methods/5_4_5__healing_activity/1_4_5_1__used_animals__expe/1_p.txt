0: Wistar albino rats (180250 g) of both sexes were selected to carry out the experiment.
1: Four rats were taken for each group.
2: The rats were used after an acclimatization period of 7 days to the laboratory environment.
3: They were housed in standard metal cages and provided with food and water ad libitum.
4: The animals were treated in accordance with the current national guidelines on animal care and use.
5: Four groups of animals containing 4 rats in each group were anaesthetized by the open mask method with anesthetic ether.
