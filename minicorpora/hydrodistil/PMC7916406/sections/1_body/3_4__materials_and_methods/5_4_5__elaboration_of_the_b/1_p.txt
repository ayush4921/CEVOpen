0: The preparation of the guaviduca beverage was carried out according to the indications of the members of the El Kiim community.
1: We placed 32 g of fresh guaviduca leaves (between two and three leaves of PCS) into 1 L of boiling water.
2: The cooking process was immediately stopped (infusion), then the leaves were mixed with the water, and allowed to cool in a closed container.
3: The container was periodically shaken lightly.
4: The infusion rested until it reached room temperature (20 C) and was then filtered through a sieve mesh (850 m) (No.
5: 20 ASTM E11).
6: The liquid fraction was stored in airtight amber containers at 4 C (refrigeration) until being used in the subsequent analysis.
