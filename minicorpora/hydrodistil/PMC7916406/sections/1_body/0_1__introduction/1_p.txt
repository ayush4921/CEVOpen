0: Guaviduca is a traditional beverage from the Ecuadorian Amazon prepared by the members of the Shuar community.
1: The guaviduca beverage is made with leaves of the  Piper carpunya species through infusion.
2: Piper carpunya is a species belonging to the Piperaceae family.
3: The Piperaceae family comprises about 2658 species belonging to 13 genera [ 1], of which the genus  Piper is the largest, made up of around 1450 species [ 2].
4: Many of these are used as culinary spices and in traditional medicine as a result of their different biological properties [ 3].
5: In Ecuador, 450 species of the Piperaceae family have been registered, of which 212 belong to the genus  Piper, with 157 native and 61 endemic varieties [ 4, 5].
