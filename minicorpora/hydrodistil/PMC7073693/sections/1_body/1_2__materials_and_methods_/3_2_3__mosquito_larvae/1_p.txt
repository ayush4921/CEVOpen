0: The colony of  Aedes aegypti used in this study was reared in the Department of Parasitology, Faculty of Pharmacy, Lac Hong University.
1: Adults were maintained in cages (30 cm  20 cm  20 cm) at a temperature of 27  3 C, with 70%  5% relative humidity under a 16:8 h light/dark cycle.
2: Mosquitoes were maintained on a 10% sucrose solution with the blood of live mice, whereas larvae were reared in plastic trays and fed with Wiskcat food (for cats).
3: The larvae between the third and fourth stages of  Aedes aegypti served as the test organisms.
