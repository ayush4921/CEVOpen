0: Nutmeg, along with its use and consumption as a spice, is often incorporated into products to reduce diarrhea, relieve joint and muscle pain, and treat scars.
1: However, there is currently no research on its use for acne treatment.
2: Therefore, the aim of this study was to develop a method to remove the safrole and myristicin of nutmeg and evaluate its application in the treatment of acne.
