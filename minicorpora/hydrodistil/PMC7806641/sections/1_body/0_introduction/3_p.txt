0: Sweet cherry ( Prunus avium L.), which is also known as large cherry, is a fleshy stone fruit that belongs to the genus  Prunus, and it is grown in specific regions with temperate climates 23.
1: The color of sweet cherry fruit is bright, and the fruit is sweet, delicious, and rich in nutrients 24.
2: In southern China, the introduction and cultivation of sweet cherries has experienced problems, such as a low rate of fruit set, serious fruit drop, unstable yield and fruit malformation, which have seriously affected the development of the sweet cherry industry in the region.
3: Some scholars have explained the effect of temperature on the flowering and fruiting of sweet cherry introduced in the southern region from the aspects of cold demand, flower bud differentiation process and rate, embryo development, and quality and fruit setting 25.
4: Lech et al.
5: 26 analyzed the flowering of several sweet cherry cultivars in the climatic conditions of southern Poland.
6: In addition, Legua et al.
7: 27 investigated the bioactive and volatile compounds in sweet cherry fruits.
8: However, related research on the VOCs of sweet cherry flowers is lacking.
9: The aroma of sweet cherry flowers is mainly derived from its essential oil.
10: In this experiment, sweet cherry flowers from four cultivars were utilized as the research object; their essential oils were extracted, which provide a reference for the utilization of the essential oil of sweet cherry flowers.
11: The identification of their VOCs will provide a scientific basis for elucidating the floral fragrance formation mechanism, metabolism and regulation.
