0: To select the sampling region, at first, habitats of the plant were identified through field surveys.
1: Then, Daran region, located in Isfahan, Iran was selected (longitude: E 46 49 02; latitude: N 36 54 170).
2: To sample the studied plant, in June 2018, coinciding with flowering, three points were selected randomly from Daran region.
3: At each point, leaves and flowers of  S. hydrangea were collected randomly from different plants (100 plants at each point).
4: The specimens were transferred to the laboratory after being harvested and then exposed to free air to dry.
5: One sample of the whole plant was also collected and pressed.
6: The specimens were identified and recorded in the herbarium of the University of Kashan.
