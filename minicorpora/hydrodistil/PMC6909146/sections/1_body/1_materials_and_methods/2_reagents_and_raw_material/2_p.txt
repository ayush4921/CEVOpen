0: Fresh  Citrus reticulate Blanco (Ponkan), originated in Luxi county of Hunan Province, China, was purchased from the local market.
1: The fruit was harvested at the end of October in it natural maturation stage.
2: The peel was peeled from fresh fruits and rinsed with clean water.
3: The peel was softly dried at 45 C in a drying oven by a flowing air for 24 h. The moisture content was about 40% by weight on a dry basis.
4: It was crushed in a plants grinder, stored and sealed at a temperature of 4 C. Wilkins-Chalgren Anaerobe Broth, Anaerobe Basal Agar, OxoidTM Tryptone, Soya peptone, and OxoidTM yeast extract were purchased from Thermo ScientificTM (Shanghai, China).
5: The beef extract was purchased from Beijing Shuangxuan Microbe Medium Products Plant (Beijing, China).
6: Cholesterol, Petroleum ether, sodium chloride, and other general reagents were purchased from Sinopharm Chemical Reagent Co., Ltd (Shanghai, China).
7: d-limonene, clindamycin, erythromycin, and tetracycline were purchased from Sinopharm Chemical Reagent Co., Ltd (Shanghai, China).
8: Staphylococcus aureus ATCC25923,  Bacillus subtilis CMCC63501,  Escherichia coli ATCC25922 were obtained from China General Microbiological Culture Collection Center (CGMCC) and preserve at the Industrial Biotechnology Laboratory in Wuhan University of Science & Technology (WUST).
9: C. acnes was isolated from the acne pus of a patient and preserve at the Industrial Biotechnology Laboratory in WUST.
