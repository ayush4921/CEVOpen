0: For morphological studies,  Sf9 cells were cultured in 96-well plates at the same density used for viability experiments, in the presence of the molecules under study.
1: After incubation, cells were washed with Hanks balanced salt solution (HBSS) and fixed in 10% formalin solution for 30 min, at room temperature.
2: CF543 (5 U/mL) and DAPI (0.25 g/mL) were added, and cells were stained for 25 min at room temperature and washed with HBSS.
