0: Essential oils of caraway seeds ( Carum Carvi L.), clove flower buds ( Eugenia carypylata L.), stems and leaves of sweet wormwood ( Artemisa annua L.) and spearmint leaves ( Mentha spicata L.) were extracted separately from 200 g samples.
1: Extraction was done through a hydro-distillation procedure of the plant materials for 3 h using a Clevenger apparatus [ 18].
2: The extracted pure essential oils were stored at 4 C in clean amber dark glass bottles until used.
