0: The pharmacological power of hemp depends on the content of 9-tetrahydrocannabinolic acid (THCA) and cannabidiolic acid (CBDA) [ 9, 10], but also on terpenes entourage effects [ 2, 11, 12].
1: Other major cannabinoids include cannabinolic acid (CBNA), cannabigerolic acid (CBGA), cannabichromenic acid (CBCA) and cannabinodiolic acid (CBNDA) [ 13, 14].
2: All these chemicals are also found in industrial hemp (with legal requirement of THC content< 0.3%).
3: The concentration of these natural products varies depending on plant genotype (cultivar, variety), the plant growth stage, growth conditions and fertilization, and plant part [ 13, 15, 16] and also on the postharvest heating treatment [ 17].
