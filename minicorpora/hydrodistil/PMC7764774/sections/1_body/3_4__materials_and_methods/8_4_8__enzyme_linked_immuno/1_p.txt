0: Blood was drawn from each rat by intracardiac puncture just before they were sacrificed.
1: The blood samples were transferred into tubes, centrifuged at 3000  g for 10 min, and dissociated to serum.
2: The acquired serum samples were kept in tubes at 80 C until analysis.
3: IL 1 and RANKL (E0119Ra and E0289Ra respectively; Bioassay Technology Laboratory, Shanghai, China) levels were analyzed using ELISA kits in accordance with the manufacturers instructions.
