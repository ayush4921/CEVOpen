0: A statistical analysis was performed using software package, SPSS (version 22.0, Chicago, IL, USA).
1: The statistical analysis of variables between the experimental groups was done using one-way ANOVA.
2: Tukeys test was done to make a pairwise comparison between groups, also, the paired sample t-test was applied to the dependent variables to obtain separate averages for the polymorphonuclear and mononuclear inflammatory cells among the groups.
3: Values were presented as mean  standard error (SE) with  p< 0.05 regarded as statistically significant.
