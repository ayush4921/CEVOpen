0: The gum collection of  P. atlantica (pistachio tree of the Atlas) was done in June and July 2017 from the Halabja region, which is 70 km far from Sulaimani city in the north of Iraq.
1: The essential oil was obtained from the gum using the hydrodistillation method; 100 g of gum was soaked in 350 mL of distilled water in a conical flask and left for 6 h using the Clevenger apparatus according to [ 37, 38].
2: Next, the essential oil was collected after 6 h of extraction.
