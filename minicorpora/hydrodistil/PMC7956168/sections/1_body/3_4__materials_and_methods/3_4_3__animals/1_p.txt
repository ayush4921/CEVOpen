0: Thirty male albino rats weighing 180220 g were obtained from Animal House, College of Pharmacy, Prince Sattam Bin Abdulaziz University (PSAU), KSA.
1: Rats were acclimatized for 1 week and maintained under 12 h light/dark cycles with standard laboratory facilities.
2: During the experimental and acclimatized period, animals were fed with a pellet diet and free access to water.
3: Instructions and Guidelines provided by the animal care unit, PSAU, KSA, were followed when performing all of the experiments.
4: The protocol was preapproved by Bio-Ethical Research Committee (BERC), PSAU (BERC-004-12-19).
