0: Right kidney sample tissues fixed in 10% neutral formalin were managed in a tissue processing machine (ASP300s, Leica Biosystems, IL, USA); each sample was embedded in paraffin wax and sliced into 45-m-thick sections.
1: Three sections of each group were chosen and stained with hematoxylin and eosin (H&E) dye, periodic acidSchiff (PAS) stain, and Massons trichrome (MT) stain [ 57].
2: All the sections of tissue were analyzed using a microscope (Olympus BX 52) for histopathological description which was recorded by a histopathologist, blinded to the experimental groups.
3: Olympus DP21 camera fixed over the microscope was used for taking photographs.
