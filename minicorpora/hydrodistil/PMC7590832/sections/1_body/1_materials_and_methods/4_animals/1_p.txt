0: Forty-two adult male Sprague Dawley rats were obtained from Laboratory Resource Unit, Faculty of Medicine, Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia (UKM).
1: Rats (180 - 200 g) were kept in polycarbonate cages with good ventilation in animal house under standard conditions (12/12 light/dark cycle at 21  2 C and 50 - 70 % humidity).
2: All animals were acclimatized to the laboratory environment for 7 days before treatment.
3: Standard rodent pellets and tap water were provided  ad libitum throughout the experimental period.
