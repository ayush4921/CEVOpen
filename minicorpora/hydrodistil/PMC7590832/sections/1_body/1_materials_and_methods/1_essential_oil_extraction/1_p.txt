4: Fresh entire leaves (10 kg) of  P. minus were subjected to hydrodistillation with 40 L of distilled water for 8 hours using a Clevenger-type apparatus as described previously (Ahmad et al., 2014[ 2]).
5: Essential oil was collected over distilled water, separated, and dried over nitrogen gas prior to storage in dark at 4 C.
