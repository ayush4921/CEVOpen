0: The dried leaves of  S. echinata (730 g) were extracted by Soxhlet with ethyl acetate (EtOAc) for 10 h. Soxhlet extraction was preferred to static maceration because, in a previous essay, the two methods were compared on analytical samples of plant material, producing the same chromatographic profile with higher yield in favor of Soxhlet.
1: A yield of 27.4 % ( w/w) was obtained.
