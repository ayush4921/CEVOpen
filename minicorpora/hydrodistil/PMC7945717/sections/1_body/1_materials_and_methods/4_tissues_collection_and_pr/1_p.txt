0: After 2 weeks of treatments, the rats were sacrificed by decapitation under general anesthesia.
1: Serum samples were collected from the blood of each rat by centrifuging at 2,700 g for 15 min.
2: Both kidneys and livers were also collected.
3: For each rat, kidney and liver specimens were homogenized with a Potter (glass-teflon) in the presence of 10 mM Tris-HCl, pH 7.4.
4: The homogenates were centrifuged at 4C at 10,000 g for 15 min using Ultra Turrax (T25, Germany).
5: Aliquots of serum and tissue supernatants were kept at 30C until analyzed.
