0: The aromatogram was made on Petri dishes filled with Muller-Hinton agar.
1: The agar is inoculated with 100  L of suspensions of 10 8 cfu/mL and then deposited on its surface of Whatman paper discs (6 mm) impregnated with 10  L of the essential oil.
2: In order for the essential oil to diffuse, Petri dishes are then closed and left at room temperature for 30 min, then incubated at 37C for 24 h.
