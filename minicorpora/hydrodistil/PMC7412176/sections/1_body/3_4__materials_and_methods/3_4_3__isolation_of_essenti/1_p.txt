0: The essential oil was isolated from air-dried leaves and flowers (in the dark, at room temperature) with hydrodistillation (HD), for 3 h in a 5 L Clevenger-type apparatus.
1: The essential oil was dried with anhydrous magnesium sulfate (MgSO 4).
2: The hydrosol was collected in a separate glass bottle.
3: A part of it was extracted by diethyl ether in order to isolate the volatile compounds.
4: The organic phase was dried over anhydrous magnesium sulfate, filtered, and reduced to a final volume.
5: Both essential oil and organic phase were stored in 18 until their analysis.
