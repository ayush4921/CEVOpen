0: The three  Conyza species were collected from Bach Ma National Park, Thue Thien Hue province (16 11 34 N, 107 51 12 E) in April 2020.
1: The plants were identified by Dr. Do Ngoc Dai and Dr.
2: Le Thi Huong.
3: Voucher specimens, LTH129 ( Conyza canadensis), LTH130 ( Conyza sumatrensis), and LTH131 ( Conyza bonariensis) have been deposited in the Pedagogical Institute of Science, Vinh University.
4: Four-kg samples of fresh aerial parts (leaves, stems, and flowers) of each of the plants were shredded and hydrodistilled for 4 h using a Clevenger-type apparatus.
