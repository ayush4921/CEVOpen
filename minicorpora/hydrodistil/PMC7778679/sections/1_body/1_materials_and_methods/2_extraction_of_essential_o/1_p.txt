0: EO was extracted from fresh leaves of  C. nardus (150 g) by hydrodistillation, using a Clevenger-type apparatus attached to a round bottom flask (3 L) with 1500 mL of deionized water (4 h).
1: Residual water in the EO was separated from the sample by freezing.
2: The yield of the EO was 0.7% (w/w).
3: EO samples were stored at 20oC until used for chemical analysis and biological assays.
