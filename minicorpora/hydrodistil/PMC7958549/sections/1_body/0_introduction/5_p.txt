0: Citrus limon belongs to the Rutaceae family with yellow edible fruits and evergreen leaves.
1: It is rich in vitamin C, and its juice is traditionally applied to treat fevers, sore throats, scurvy, chest pain, and high blood pressure (KlimekSzczykutowicz et al.,  2020).
2: The  C. limon essential oil contains limonene (the main component of the essential oil), pinene, terpinene, sabinene, linalool, myrcene, geranial, and so forth.
3: The limoneneloaded edible coatings have been used as antimicrobial and antioxidant agents to increase the shelflife different food products (Dhital et al.,  2018; Maleki et al.,  2018).
