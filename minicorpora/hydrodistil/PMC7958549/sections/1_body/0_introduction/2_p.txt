0: Buffalo meat is considered as an alternative source of meat and a healthy choice due to the absence of hormones and stimulants.
1: It is also rich in iron and protein and has lower contents of fat and cholesterol in comparison with skinless chicken, pork, and beef meats.
2: It is also worth noting that buffalo meat has a red brilliant colour because of very low contents of intramuscular fat.
3: However, buffalo meat, as other types of fresh meat, is a very perishable food.
4: And the presence of spoilage and pathogenic microorganisms on the surface of buffalo meat could affect safety, hygienic quality, and shelflife of the product (Cannarsi et al.,  2008).
5: Furthermore, buffalo meat (raw and cooked) could undergo lipid oxidation and subsequent deterioration in colour, odor, and flavour quality and nutritional value (Descalzo et al.,  2008; Juarez et al.,  2010).
