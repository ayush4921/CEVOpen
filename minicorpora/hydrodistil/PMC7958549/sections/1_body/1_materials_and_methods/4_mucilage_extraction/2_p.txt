0: The mucilage of  P. major seeds (PMSM) was extracted based on the optimized method of Alizadeh Behbahani, Yazdi, Shahidi, Hesarinejad, et al.
1: ( 2017), according to the following conditions: water to seed ratio of 60:1, pH 6.8, temperature 75C, and 1.5hr extraction time.
2: The PMSM was collected from the seed surfaces  via an extractor, filtered, dried (45C overnight), milled, sieved, and kept under cool and dry conditions.
