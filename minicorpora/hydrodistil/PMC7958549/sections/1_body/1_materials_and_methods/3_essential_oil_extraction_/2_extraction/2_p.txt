0: The peels of  C. limon fruits were dried at room temperature and then powdered by means of a miller.
1: After that, the peel powder (50 g) was transferred to a Clevenger apparatus containing 750 ml distilled water and the hydrodistillationbased extraction process was completed (3 hr).
2: The obtained  C. limon essential oil (CLEO) was collected in glass vials and then stored at 4C (Alizadeh Behbahani, Shahidi, et al.,  2017).
