0: Hyssopus officinalis L. (Lamiaceae), also known as hyssop, is a perennial plant used as a culinary, medicinal, and essential oil crop, collected in the wild but also cultivated in Asia, Europe, and America [ 19].
1: H. officinalis EO is widely used in cosmetics, food, and pharmaceutical industries worldwide.
2: Extracts of the species have a wide range of activities, such as antioxidant, antifungal, antimicrobial, and anti-inflammatory activity, they are used for appetite stimulation, and against chronic bronchitis, for treatment of asthma, rheumatic pains, bruises, wounds, anxiety, relaxation of muscles, and so forth [ 19, 20, 21, 22].
