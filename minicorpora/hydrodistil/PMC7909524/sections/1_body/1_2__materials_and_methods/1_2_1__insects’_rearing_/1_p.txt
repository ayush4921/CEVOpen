0: The larvae of  G. pyloalis were handpicked from a mulberry orchard within the University of Guilan campus, Rasht (37.2682 N, 49.5891 E), Iran.
1: The larvae were maintained on fresh leaves of Shin Ichinoise mulberry variety in disposable transparent containers (high-density polyethylene plastic containers, 10  20  5 cm) in a rearing room set at 25  1 C, 75  5% RH (Relative Humidity), and 16:8 L:D (Light:Dark).
2: The emerging adults were reserved in glass jars (18  7  5 cm), in which fresh leaves were positioned for egg laying, and 10% honey-soaked cotton wool was provided for feeding.
