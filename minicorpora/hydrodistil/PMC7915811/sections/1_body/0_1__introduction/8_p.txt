0: Nutmeg has antioxidant and antimicrobial properties which can be used to protect chewable gel tablets from mold (fungal growth).
1: It has an effect on  Bacillus subtilis,  Staphylococcus aureus,  Shigella dysteriae,  Aspergillus niger, also,  Myristica fragrans extracts prepared in different conditions have many other activities against other bacteria [ 22, 28, 29, 30, 31].
2: Additionally, it was determined that nutmeg essential oil has effect on suppressed  Enterococcus faecalis,  Streptococcus mutans (referent), and  Pasteurella multocida bacteria [ 32].
3: Talking about  Enterococcus faecalis, it is related to oral diseases, such as caries, endodontic infections, periodontitis, and peri-implantitis [ 33, 34].
4: Using natural essential oil or other materials from nature can help reduce bacterial growth.
5: Antioxidant compounds in nutmeg seeds are: myristphenone, phenolic volatile compounds, phenolic acid (caffeic acid), flavanols (catechin).
6: Compared with other spices and herbs, nutmeg has more than 50% activity of inhibition of DPPH (2,2-diphenyl-1-picrylhydrazyl) [ 22, 35].
7: Nutmeg can not only preserve a product and prolong its shelf life (which is very important for chewable gel tablets that have water in their composition) but also it decreases bacterial growth in mouth and binds free radicals, this adds additional benefits.
