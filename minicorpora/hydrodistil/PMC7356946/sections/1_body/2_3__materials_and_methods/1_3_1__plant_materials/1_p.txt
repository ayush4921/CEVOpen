0: The above-ground parts of  T. polium and  T. decussatus were collected from Wadi Jibaal in St Katherine Protectorate (283232 N 335730.2 E), south Sinai, Egypt, during the flowering stage.
1: Voucher specimens (SK-105 ( T. polium) and SK122 ( T. decussatus)) have been deposited in the herbarium of the National Research Centre, Cairo, Egypt.
2: The collection took place under the permission of the St Katherine Protectorate for scientific purposes.
3: The specimens were authenticated by Dr. Ahmed Abd-ElGawad, Associate Professor of Plant Ecology, Faculty of Science, Mansoura University, Egypt.
