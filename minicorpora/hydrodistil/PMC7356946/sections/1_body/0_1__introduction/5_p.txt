0: Continuing our target for finding eco-friendly natural bioactive compounds [ 6, 7, 12, 21, 28, 29, 30, 31], our study aimed to (1) characterize the chemical profiles of the EOs of two medicinal plants belonging to Family Lamiaceae,  T. polium and  T. decussatus, collected from the Saint Katherine Protectorate, Sinai Peninsula, Egypt, (2) evaluate the phytotoxic effects of the extracted EOs on  Lactuca sativa (lettuce), (2) determine the antimicrobial activities of EOs on different microorganisms, and (4) evaluate the significance of the extraction method on the chemical composition via chemometrics analysis.
