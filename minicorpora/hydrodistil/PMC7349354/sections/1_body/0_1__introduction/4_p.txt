0: The granary weevil,  Sitophilus granaries, is a cosmopolitan insect pest of storage facilities and processing plants.
1: Sitophilus granarius causes damage to beans, corn, sorghum, nuts, oats, peanuts, rice, and wheat [ 30, 31].
2: This pest is controlled with synthetic insecticides, such as phosphine, which is highly efficacious against  S. granarius.
3: However, phosphine resistance has been reported in some populations of  S. granarius [ 32].
