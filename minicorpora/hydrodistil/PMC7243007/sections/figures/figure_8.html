In vitro effect of oregano on the linearity (LIN). The dilution of EO was done in a 0.2% ( w/ v) sterile agar solution. The percentage of LIN of each aliquot was measured after the addition of the EO and 5 min and 10 min treatment. Oregano oil could significantly improve LIN over a period of 5 to 10 min ( ∗ P < 0.05; n = 25).
