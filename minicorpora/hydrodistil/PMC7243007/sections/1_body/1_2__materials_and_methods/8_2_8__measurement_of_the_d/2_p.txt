0: The sperm sample was washed twice in phosphate-buffered saline (PBS, Sigma-Aldrich, Gillingham, UK) and adjusted to a concentration of 2  10 7 cells/ml in PBS.
1: The cell suspension was then fixed in PBS containing 2% formaldehyde (Sigma-Aldrich) for 60 min at room temperature.
2: Following a double wash with PBS, the sample is centrifuged at 1200 rpm.
3: This step is repeated twice.
4: Slides prepared were immediately analyzed using a fluorescence microscope (Nikon Eclipse 80i) equipped with appropriate filters.
5: The images were captured using a CCD camera and XytoGen software (Excilone, version 3.8.46, France) ( Figure 2) [ 7].
