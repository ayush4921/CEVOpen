0: The experiment was set up over an area of 500 m 2 for each plant species and it included the following variants: Npnoninfested peppermint plants (control); Ipinfested peppermint plants; Ncnoninfested chamomile plants (control); Icinfested chamomile plants.
1: There were five plots in each variant and the plot size was 6.25  4 m (25 m 2), while the distance between plots was 2 m. A wooden quadrat frame (50 cm  50 cm) was used for sampling.
2: Ten quadrats (two per plot) were sampled for each variant of the experiment.
3: Samples collected from all 10 quadrats were added together and considered to constitute a representative sample of the community.
