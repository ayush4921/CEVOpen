0: Microelectrode-APs were recorded using glass microelectrodes (filled with 3 M KCl), which were inserted in the left and right ventricular (LV and RV) walls from the epicardial surface.
1: The recorded APs were amplified and digitized by the 16 channel PowerLab system (ADInstruments, Oxford, UK) at a frequency of 20 kHz.
