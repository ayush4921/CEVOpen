0: Coriander oil was obtained from GAL (Poznan, Poland), which specializes in production of cold pressed oils for pharmaceutical and nutraceutical purposes.
1: The oil was pressed in the GAL factory from coriander fruit, a week before experiments were carried out.
2: Standards of terpenes and fatty acids used in the experiments were purchased from Sigma-Aldrich (Sigma Aldrich, Poznan, Poland).
3: Basic, chemical characteristics of coriander oil were determined in the GAL laboratory (peroxide value (PV), fatty acids methyl esters (FAME) profile).
