0: Coriander essential oil was obtained using a traditional hydrodistillation method, using a Deryng apparatus.
1: To a 1 L round flask, 400 mL of water was added and 100 mL of coriander cold pressed oil.
2: The flask was heated and boiling water provided steam to distill off essential oil from cold pressed oil.
3: A total of 7.8 mL of essential oil was collected after 2 h distillation.
