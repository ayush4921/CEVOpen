0: Euphorbia intisy Drake, known locally as Herotse or Horotsy, is a cactiform shrub of the Southern Malagasy dry forest attaining 4 to 7 m. It belongs to the section Pervilleanae of the Euphorbiaceae that groups together seven species which are strictly Malagasy [ 30].
1: Trunk is upright and branched at the base.
2: Young stems are dichotomously branched with leaves reduced to scales (almost leafless).
3: Root system are swollen displaying a strategy to store water in xeric conditions [ 33].
4: E. intisy was nearly made extinct by massive exploitation in the beginning of the 20th century [ 36].
5: It is now classified as Near Threatened according to the IUCN red list [ 37].
