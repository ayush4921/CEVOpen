0: The poultry industry worldwide is one of the main agricultural subsectors [ 1].
1: The supplementation of poultry diets with natural products containing bioactive components has shown promising results [ 2, 3, 4, 5, 6].
2: The admission of essential oils (EO) extracted from aromatic plants in the formation of poultry rations could be a promising alternative to antibiotics that is safer for the environment.
3: Essential oil extracts are widely used for skin care [ 7], aromatherapy, beauty treatments, herbal medicines and perfumery applications [ 8].
4: Aromatic plants and their essential oil extracts possess potential insecticidal activities and antimicrobial effects against pathogens [ 9, 10, 11, 12], stimulating the effect on the digestive system [ 13].
5: These herbs and their extracts received more attention as possible antibiotic growth promoter (AGP) alternatives due to being natural, easily available, non-toxic and residue-free.
