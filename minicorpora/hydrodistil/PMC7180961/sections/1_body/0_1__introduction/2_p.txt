0: The genus  Achillea includes over a hundred species and subspecies, which are distributed in the Northern hemisphere [ 9].
1: The use of the inflorescence, as well as the whole plant, of many of these species has a long-established role in folk medicine to treat a wide range of ailments that mainly affect the digestive system.
2: It takes various application forms, which differ depending on the geographical area [ 10, 11, 12, 13].
3: Besides their medicinal properties, some  Achillea species are also cultivated as ornamental plants.
