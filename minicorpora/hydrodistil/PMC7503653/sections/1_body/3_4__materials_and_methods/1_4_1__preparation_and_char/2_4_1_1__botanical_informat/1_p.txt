0: Capitiu ( Siparuna guianensis) belongs to the  Siparunaceae Family.
1: It is a shrub about three meters high, the immature fruits were greenish, and the ripe ones were greenish and purplish, with short pedunculated axillary racemes, and opposite, elliptic, and lanceolate leaves.
2: This plant releases a characteristic odour of fish.
3: Leaf samples were identified and deposited in the medicinal plants herbarium of the Museu Paraense Emilio Goeldi.
