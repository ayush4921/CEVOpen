0: Investigation of the pharmacological activities of essential oils of different  Boswellia ssp.
1: resins reveals a wide range of medicinal uses.
2: In particular,  B. sacra has been used as an analgesic, antioxidant, cardio protective substance and an anti-inflammatory [ 2, 3, 4].
3: Frankincense essential oils have been commonly used to treat microbial infections from as early as the 11th century, and some authors have studied the effect of frankincense on urinary tract infections [ 5].
4: Resin essential oils have an antimicrobial activity against important human pathogens, both bacterial and fungal organisms, such as  Staphylococcus aureus,  Escherichia coli,  Proteus vulgaris and  Candida albicans [ 6, 7].
5: B. sacra oil also shows significant activity on  Aspergillus parasiticus and  Aspergillus flavus growth, and consequently suppresses aflatoxin production [ 8].
