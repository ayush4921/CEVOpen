0: Omani resin is commercially available in  B. sacra different accessions, under the local names Houjri, Najdi, and Sahli or Shaebi ( Figure 1), based on different geographic locations in Dhofar from where the resins are harvested.
1: Houjri, the first grade resin, has the lightest color and a larger clump size, is collected from trees growing in the north of the Samhan mountains, and is the most expensive.
2: Najdi, the second resin, has a pale yellow color and is collected from the plateau behind the Dhofar mountains.
3: Finally, Sahli or Shaebi, which also has a darker color, is collected from the valleys and is the cheaper one.
