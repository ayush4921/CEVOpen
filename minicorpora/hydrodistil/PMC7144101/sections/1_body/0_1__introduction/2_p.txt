0: Magnolia grandiflora L. (Magnoliaceae) is a large evergreen tree native to North America [ 9] that has medicinal and ornamental values.
1: Medicinal use of various parts of  M. grandiflora is reported in American Indian medicine and also listed as a bitter tonic and antimalarial.
2: Several biologically active compounds have been reported from  Magnolia species [ 10, 11, 12].
3: As a part of our natural product screening program for mosquitoes, we tested essential oils from various parts of  M. grandiflora for their larvicidal and biting deterrent activities.
4: This paper reports insecticidal and biting deterrent activities of essential oils and select pure compounds from various parts of  M. grandiflora against yellow fever mosquito,  Aedes aegypti.
