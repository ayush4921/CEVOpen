0: All data were analyzed using Bio-Rad CFX Manager software (Bio-Rad).
1: The quantification of number of parasites was performed considering the standard deviation (SD) of Cq values, excluding SD >0.05.
2: The average of values Starting Quantity (SQ) per sample was used to plot graphics seen in results.
3: Statistical analysis was done by GraphPad (GraphPad Prism 8, California, USA).
4: The Shapiro-Wilk test was used to access data normality.
5: Differences and comparisons among groups were performed by one-way ANOVA or Kruskal-Wallis test, followed by Dunnetts multiple comparisons test.
