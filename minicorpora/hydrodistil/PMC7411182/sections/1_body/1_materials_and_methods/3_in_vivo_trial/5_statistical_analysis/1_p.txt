0: The basic study design was RCB (random complete block design) and the replication (pen) was considered as the experimental unit.
1: Experimental data were analyzed by one-way ANOVA (general linear model) function of the SPSS statistical package (version 20.0) ( 39).
2: Microbiology data were log-transformed (log10) before analysis.
3: Data homogeneity was tested using Levene's test.
4: Significance level was set at 5% ( p  0.05).
5: Values of  p between 5 and 10% (0.05<  p  0.10) were reported as trends.
