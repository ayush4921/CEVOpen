0: The 4 kg sunflower receptacles were collected from Daan City, Jilin Province (1231245 E, 445223 N) in October 2019 and identified by Professor Shuwen Guan, School of Life Science, Jilin University.
1: The samples were air-dried and then crushed into powder.
2: In each experiment, 100 g powder was extracted by hydrodistillation in Clevenger-type apparatus for 8 h [ 27].
3: The experiments of extraction were repeated 30 times to provide enough SEO for analysis of biological activities.
4: SEO was stored at 4 C until analysis.
