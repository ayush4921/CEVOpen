0: Airdried plants of cultivated  Thymus vulgaris and  Thymbra spicata were obtained from Diyarbakir Research Institute (July 2017), Turkey.
1: A NEOS microwave extraction system (MA 125 Milestone, Italy) was used for this study.
2: Seventyfive grams of dry plant material was weighed into a beaker.
3: Then, 150 ml of distilled water was added (1:2, w/v).
4: After 30 min of resting, extraction was performed at 550 W power for 30 min.
5: Then, the essential oil was collected and placed into vial with anhydrous sodium sulfate to remove any water.
6: Then, vials were stored at 4C for later analysis.
