0: Hydrodistillation was conducted using an ElectromantleTM (EM2000 CE, Electrothermal Engineering Ltd., UK, 500 W).
1: Seventyfive grams of airdried plant material was weighed into a round flask.
2: Then, 750 ml of distilled water (1:10, w/v) was added.
3: Extraction was performed for three hours.
4: Next, the essential oil was collected and placed into a vial with anhydrous sodium sulfate to remove any water.
5: Then, vials were stored at 4C for later analysis.
