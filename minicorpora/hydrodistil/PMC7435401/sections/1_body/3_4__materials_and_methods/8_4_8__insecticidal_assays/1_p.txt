0: The larvicidal effect of the EOs was evaluated according to standard procedures established by the WHO [ 50].
1: Three different hemp EOs were analyzed: (i) Felina 32 dioecious inflorescence, (ii) CS female inflorescence, (iii) CS male inflorescence.
2: For the treatment of mosquitoes, the EOs were dissolved in dimethyl sulfoxide (DMSO) 1:10.
3: Twenty-five larvae L3 (third instar stage) and twenty-five pupae were placed in plastic cups containing water.
4: Treatment was carried out using different dilutions of the three essential oils preparations: 50, 75, 100, and 125 ppm.
5: Control groups were prepared adding pure breeding water or DMSO (200 L).
6: Mortality of larvae and pupae was observed after 24 h. Three independent experiments were performed.
