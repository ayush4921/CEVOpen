0: Plants have a long history of therapeutic use in the management of diseases since time immemorial.
1: Essential oils are natural, complex, and aromatic volatile compounds produced by plants, and they are generally present at low concentrations.
2: These compounds possess antimicrobial and antioxidant activities, and they function in the treatment of various kinds of diseases [ 1].
3: They are also beneficial in various food industries (for preserving food against oxidation), as alternatives to artificial chemicals in cosmetics and perfumery industries for the production of various cologne waters, bathing lotions, hair lotions, and shampoos, and as components of disinfectants and insecticides [ 2].
