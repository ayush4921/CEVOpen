0: Tribolium confusum and  Sitophilus zeamais were taken from the Laboratory of Entomology, Regional Research Center on Horticulture and Organic Agriculture, Chott-Mariem (CRRHAB), Tunisia.
1: Insect adults were cultured in a growth cabinet set at the following rearing conditions: 28  1 C, 60% relative humidity (RI), 16 h light and 8 h dark photoperiod, without exposure to any insecticidal contamination.
2: The food media used were wheat flour for  T.  confusum and whole maize grains for  S.  zeamais.
