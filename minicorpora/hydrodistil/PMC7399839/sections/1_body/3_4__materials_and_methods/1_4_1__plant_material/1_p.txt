0: The plant material was collected from Zoresti, near Buzau, Romania, at the altitude of around 116 m (451054 N 264210 E) in late July 2014 by a local supplier.
1: The aerial parts of  Salvia officinalis and the leaves from  Satureja hortensis and  Anethum graveolens (separated from branches) were dried at room temperature and manually grounded.
2: Their taxonomic affiliations were confirmed by Dr. Gheorghe Irina from the Botanical Garden Dimitrie Brandza, University of Bucharest.
