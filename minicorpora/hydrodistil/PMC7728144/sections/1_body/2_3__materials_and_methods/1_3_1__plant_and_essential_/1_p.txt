0: Aerial parts (leaves and stems) of  M. leucadendra plant were collected during early hours of the morning in March 2015 at National Botany Garden (NBG), Havana, Cuba, and a specimen was deposited at the Herbarium of Cuban Flora of the NBG and authenticated by M.Sc.
1: Eldys Becquer (voucher number: 8501918).
2: Leaves of  M. leucadendra were manually selected, rinsed with abundant water, and crushed into small pieces.
3: Immediately, the fresh vegetal material was conventionally hydrodistilled using a Clevenger-type apparatus for 5 h, and the EO-ML was obtained, yielding 0.8%.
4: The essential oil was stored in a sealed amber vial at 4 C until analysis and screening.
