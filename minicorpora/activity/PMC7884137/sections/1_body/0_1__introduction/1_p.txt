0: Ulcerative colitis (UC) is a form of inflammatory bowel disease characterized by the intestine chronic inflammation [ 1].
1: The prevalence and incidence of UC is on the rise and is considered a global disease [ 2].
2: UC is a known risk factor for intestinal cancer, especially for colorectal cancer [ 3,  4].
3: At present, the main treatments include anti-inflammatory drugs, steroids, biologicals, immunomodulators, and so on.
4: But, these drugs can only alleviate symptoms and prolong phases of remission.
5: Moreover, some severe cases do not respond well to the treatments.
6: Therefore, some alternative therapies are urgently needed [ 5].
