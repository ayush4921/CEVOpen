0: The current pandemic that struck the world in December 2019 and is ongoing till this day, has turned into the deadliest pandemic after influenza pandemic that took place in 1918.
1: This is the third time that a coronavirus has caused an outbreak during the 21st century.
2: The recent one is caused by the novel coronavirus termed as the severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2) by the World Health Organization (WHO) and is responsible for a respiratory disease named as the coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19).
3: However, COVID-19 has turned out to be more deadly than the previously spread SARS and MERS (Middle east respiratory syndrome) coronaviruses.
4: Owing to this, 1.30 million people have died because of COVID-19 and 53.76 million have tested positive for the virus as of November 13, 2020 ( Worldometer 2020).
5: The reason that it took only over two months to be declared as pandemic is the interconnectedness and ever increased worldwide travel.
