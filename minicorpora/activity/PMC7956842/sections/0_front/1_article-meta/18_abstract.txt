0: Even though several plants can improve the female reproductive function, the use of herbs, herbal preparations, or essential oils during pregnancy is questionable.
1: This review is focused on the effects of some essential oils and their constituents on the female reproductive system during pregnancy and on the development of the fetus.
2: The major concerns include causing abortion, reproductive hormone modulation, maternal toxicity, teratogenicity, and embryo-fetotoxicity.
3: This work summarizes the important studies on the reproductive effects of essential oil constituents anethole, apiole, citral, camphor, thymoquinone,   trans-sabinyl acetate, methyl salicylate, thujone, pulegone, -elemene, -eudesmol, and costus lactone, among others.
