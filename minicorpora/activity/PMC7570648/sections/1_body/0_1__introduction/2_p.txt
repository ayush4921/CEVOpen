0: The consumption of herbal infusions is very common in the Mediterranean region and globally.
1: In a study conducted on 1260 cancer patients in Palestine, 60.9% were consuming herbs, mostly in the form of decoctions [ 10].
2: These drinks are mainly prepared from aromatic plants belonging to the following families: Lauracae, Umbelliferae, Lamiaceae, Myrtacae and Compositae [ 3].
3: The plants used in the preparation of herbal infusions were subjected to several studies and some of these plants exhibited potent antioxidant and anticancer properties.
4: However, overconsumption of these herbal infusions may result in contradictory and side effects.
5: This review summarizes the antioxidant capacities, anticancer potential, and possible toxicity of eleven widely-consumed herbal infusions.
