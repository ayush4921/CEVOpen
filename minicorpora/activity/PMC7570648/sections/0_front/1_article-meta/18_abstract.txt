0: The release of reactive oxygen species (ROS) and oxidative stress is associated with the development of many ailments, including cardiovascular diseases, diabetes and cancer.
1: The causal link between oxidative stress and cancer is well established and antioxidants are suggested as a protective mechanism against cancer development.
2: Recently, an increase in the consumption of antioxidant supplements was observed globally.
3: The main sources of these antioxidants include fruits, vegetables, and beverage.
4: Herbal infusions are highly popular beverages consumed daily for different reasons.
5: Studies showed the potent antioxidant effects of plants used in the preparation of some herbal infusions.
6: Such herbal infusions represent an important source of antioxidants and can be used as a dietary protection against cancer.
7: However, uncontrolled consumption of herbal infusions may cause toxicity and reduced antioxidant activity.
8: In this review, eleven widely consumed herbal infusions were evaluated for their antioxidant capacities, anticancer potential and possible toxicity.
9: These herbal infusions are highly popular and consumed as daily drinks in different countries.
10: Studies discussed in this review will provide a solid ground for researchers to have better understanding of the use of herbal infusions to reduce oxidative stress and as protective supplements against cancer development.
