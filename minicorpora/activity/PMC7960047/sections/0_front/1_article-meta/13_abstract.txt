0: Ximenia americana is one of the most valuable wild edible plants in the world.
1: In different countries, it is utilized as food, medicine, an essential oil source, and the industrial component to other products.
2: In Ethiopia, it was one of the most known and very important plants for a long period of time.
3: It was utilized as food, a medicinal plant, and animal feed.
4: It was also one of the most economically important and culturally valuable plants.
5: But nowadays, it is not adequately available in the country due to deforestation problem in the years.
6: In addition, its economic importance, current status, and medicinal roles are not well documented and understood.
7: As for research studies, it is concluded that unless a collective effort is taken, the existence of this plant is under severe threat and needs to have some measures.
8: This review article is aimed at addressing the abovelined topics in detail and to pinpoint and explain the importance and status of   Ximenia americana.
