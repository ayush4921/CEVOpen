0: Psidium genus belongs to Myrtaceae, Myrtales order, Rosidae clade, Malvidae subclade, and comprises about 266 species with occurrence in the tropical and subtropical regions of the world.
1: In the Americas, it extends from southern Mexico to Argentina.
2: The centers of high diversity of  Psidium species are western India, southern Brazil, Paraguay, and northern South America [ 1, 2, 3].
