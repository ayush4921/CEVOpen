0: Raspberry (  Rubus idaeus L.) seed oil (RSO) is considered as a source of high value bioactive compounds as fatty acids, tocopherols, tocotrienols, carotenoids, flavonoids, phytosterols, antioxidants, monoterpenes and many other chemical constituents.
1: These compounds are appreciated as a source of nutrition for humans, as additives in cosmetic production, has immense therapeutic potential.
2: Raspberry seed oil exerts many pharmacological effects included antimicrobial, antioxidant, anti-inflammatory activity and many other effects.
3: The various databases like PubMed and Science Direct were used to identify, analyze and summarize the research literature on raspberries.
4: This review will highlight recent developments of the chemical constituents and nutraceutical and cosmetical effects of RSO.
5: Practical application: analyzed recent researches and international patents containing raspberry seed oil can help practitioners of various industries create new high-value products.
