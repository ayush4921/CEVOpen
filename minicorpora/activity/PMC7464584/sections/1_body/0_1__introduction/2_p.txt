0: Plants belonging to  Argemone genus are important medicinal plants [ 13].
1: Several traditional uses were described from these plants, such as expectorant, demulcent, diuretic, emetic, and treatment in chronic skin diseases [ 14].
2: The oils from  A. mexicana seeds were widely used in the treatment of several diseases like intestinal infections, ulcers, dysentery, asthma, and hypertension [ 15, 16, 17].
3: Leaves, seeds, and flowers of  A. mexicana were stated to have numerous medicinal uses, such as coughs, and maintenance of blood cholesterol and normal circulation as well as anti-venom [ 18, 19, 20, 21].
4: The chemical characterization of  Argemone plant species afforded various metabolites, such as terpenoids, alkaloids, phenolics, and flavonoids [ 14].
