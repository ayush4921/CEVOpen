0: Babassu is a palm tree endemic from Brazil.
1: Its oil is used in the manufacturing of food, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals, being the main product from extractive sources used in the industry in the world [ 1].
2: The harvesting of babassu fruits is a predominantly women activity, which are known as coconut breakers women.
3: It is the primary source of income for more than 300,000 people in Northeast Brazil, mainly in the Maranhao State, in the borders of the Amazon rain forest [ 2] and also in other more isolated areas, as in the Araripe Region [ 3].
