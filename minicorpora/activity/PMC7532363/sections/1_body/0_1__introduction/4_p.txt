0: Despite the widespread use of medicinal plants and their derived products in the treatment of skin wounds in folk medicine and the recognition that phytochemicals can have positive effects on wound healing [ 14], the effect of babassu oil on skin wounds has not been scientifically evaluated until now.
1: Thus, this study aimed investigates in vitro and in vivo the wound healing effects of babassu oil as well as evaluate its anti-inflammatory activity on chronic skin lesions.
