0: Ageratum conyzoides L. is an annual herb with a long history in its benefits as a traditional medicine in many countries in the world.
1: This weed has been known since the past time for its medicinal effect in various diseases such as common wound and the burned one, antimicrobe, arthrosis, headache, and dyspnea.
2: The further medicinal effect has been reported such as antipneumonia, pain killer, anti-inflammatory agent, antiasthma, antispasmodic, haemostatic, gastrointestinal disorder, gynaecological disorder, antileprosy, and many other skin diseases [ 3].
