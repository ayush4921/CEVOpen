0: In recent years, Romanian entrepreneurs have focused on cultivating aromatic and medicinal plants.
1: They focused especially on plants that are found in the Mediterranean area.
2: If until a few years ago we met many crops of vegetables or plants specific to the Romanian area, in recent years many crops of lavender, fennel, mint or basil have appeared.
3: The lavender culture is found in the plain and depression area of Romania because the plant needs a warm and moderately dry climate.
4: The fennel culture is most often found in the southern part of Romania, where it is mostly plain and there is a warm climate.
5: The mint culture is the most common in the depression area of Romania because it is a plant that has a moderate tolerance to drought, requiring numerous irrigations.
6: Basil crops are most often found in southern Romania, more precisely in the plains because the plant prefers a warm climate.
7: Basil is in first place at the top of Romanians preferences in terms of cultivating aromatic and medicinal plants [ 47, 48, 49, 50, 51].
