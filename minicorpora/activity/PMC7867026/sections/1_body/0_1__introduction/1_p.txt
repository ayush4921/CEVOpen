0: Eugenia uniflora L. (Myrtaceae), popularly known in Brazil as pitangueira, is a small tree species widely distributed in South and Central America [ 1, 2].
1: In view of the pharmacological activities attributed to the species in folk medicine, the plant has received intense scientific attention and become of interest to the cosmetic industry [ 3, 4, 5].
2: E. uniflora currently has at least 41 recognized and registered heterotypic synonyms in Brazil [ 6], which indicates the existence of a large number of varieties for this plant species.
