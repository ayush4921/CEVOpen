0: Cannabinoids bind to cannabinoid receptors CB 1 and CB 2 in humans [ 11], and are distributed throughout the body, including in immune cells.
1: It has been suggested that activation of these receptors could modulate the function of the immune system during infection [ 16].
2: At least 113 different cannabinoids have been isolated from  C. sativa, many of which exhibit various pharmacological effects [ 17].
3: One of the most studied and main intoxicating cannabinoids is  9-THC, which exerts its effects through partial agonism at the CB 1 receptor [ 18, 19].
4: Another major constituent is CBD, but this compound is not intoxicating [ 18].
5: Most cannabinoids are metabolized in the liver by cytochrome P450 [ 20].
