( A, B) Effect of carvacrol and thymol combination in cell death induction through non-apoptotic pathways. Flow cytometric analysis of cell death with Annexin V-APC/7-AAD double staining after 48 h treatment of KG1 and HL60 cell lines with the combination of 50 µM Thy/200 µM Cr and K562 cell line with 75 µM Thy/300 µM Cr, in the presence of the pan caspase inhibitor Z-VAD ( A) or with the ROS scavenger NAC ( B).
