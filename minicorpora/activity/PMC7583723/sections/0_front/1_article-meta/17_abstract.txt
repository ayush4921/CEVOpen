0: Trypanosomiases are diseases caused by parasitic protozoan trypanosomes of the genus   Trypanosoma.
1: In humans, this includes Chagas disease and African trypanosomiasis.
2: There are few therapeutic options, and there is low efficacy to clinical treatment.
3: Therefore, the search for new drugs for the trypanosomiasis is urgent.
4: This review describes studies of the trypanocidal properties of essential oils, an important group of natural products widely found in several tropical countries.
5: Seventy-seven plants were selected from literature for the trypanocidal activity of their essential oils.
6: The main chemical constituents and mechanisms of action are also discussed.
7: In vitro and in vivo experimental data show the therapeutic potential of these natural products for the treatment of infections caused by species of   Trypanosoma.
