0: The seed of this plant is used in ancient medicine for a number of ailments including back pain, asthma, fever, bronchitis, cough, chest congestion, dizziness, paralysis, chronic headache, inflammation, infertility, and other gastrointestinal disorders like dyspepsia, flatulence, diarrhea, and dysentery ( Durmuskahya and Ozturk, 2013;  Gholamnezhad et al., 2016;  Nasir et al., 2014;  Shah, 1966).
1: Additionally,  N. sativa seed oil is used for the remedy of an abscess, nasal ulcer, swollen joint, orchitis and eczema.
2: N. sativa oil is also used in combination with honey for asthmatic problems, bronchospasms and chest congestion ( Gholamnezhad et al., 2016;  Nasir et al., 2014).
3: In ancient literature,  N. sativa is also attributed as an analgesic, liver tonic, diuretic, appetite stimulant, analgesic and digestive ( Gilani et al., 2004;  Rajsekhar and Kuldeep, 2011;  Tavakkoli et al., 2017;  Ziaee et al., 2012).
