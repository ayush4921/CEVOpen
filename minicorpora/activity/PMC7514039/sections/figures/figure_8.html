Effect of light treatments of four precursor metabolites involved in the shikimic acid and cinnamic acid pathways. Shikimic acid (A), phenylalanine (B), cinnamic acid (C) and p-coumaric acid (D) contents in root, leaf and whole A. heterotropoides var. mandshuricum grown in different light irradiations. Note: I, 100% full sunlight; II, 50% full sunlight; III, 24% full sunlight; IV, 12% full sunlight. Error bar represents standard deviation (n = 5); different letters on the bars mean significant difference ( P <0.05).
