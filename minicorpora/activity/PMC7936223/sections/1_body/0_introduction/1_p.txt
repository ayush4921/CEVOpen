0: Over the years, the use of prophylactic drugs and antibiotic growth promoters in animal feeding has become commonplace.
1: However, the continuous use of these compounds resulted in outcomes such as the emergence of resistant bacteria and antibiotic residues in meat and other livestock products which pose a risk to public health and the environment ( Jazi et al., 2018a).
2: This concern has led many countries to restrict the use of antibiotics in animal feed.
3: Accordingly, it is necessary to identify cost-effective, safe, and eco-friendly alternatives to antibiotics.
