0: Larvae of  An.
1: stephensi are found in a wide variety of breeding habitats such as fresh and brackish waters in rural, coastal and urban areas.
2: In rural areas, the larvae exploit freshwater pools, stream banks and bottoms, catch basins, seepage canals, wells, and local water storage containers.
3: In urban areas, they readily breed in numerous artificial containers inside and outside homes, as well as in industrial regions [ 9,  13,  14].
