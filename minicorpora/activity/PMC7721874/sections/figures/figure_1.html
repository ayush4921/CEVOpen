Chemical composition of Satureja hortensis essential oil. Unheated and heated essential oils chromatograms (1-α-thujene, 2-α-pinene, 3-sabinene, 4-β-pinene, 5-α-terpinolene, 6- p-cymene, 7-γ-terpinene, 8-carvacrol, 9-caryophyllene, 10-β-bisabolene) obtained by GC–MS. Annotations. Satureja hortensis essential oil denoted as SHEO and heated essential oils at different temperatures and periods denoted as SHEOx-y, where x is the temperature of treatment (ºC), and y is the heating time in hours (SHEO160-0.5, SHEO175-0.5, SHEO175-2.5, and SHEO190-2.5).
