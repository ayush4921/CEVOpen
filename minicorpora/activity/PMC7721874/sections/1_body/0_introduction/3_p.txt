0: Saturejahortensis is one of the typical plants of the  Lamiaceae family, and its essential oil has a wide variation of composition depending on the growing area and climate conditions.
1: As found in literature 5,  7  11 the main components of  S.hortensis essential oil are carvacrol,  -terpinene,  p-cymene, thymol, caryophyllene,  -terpinolene,  -pinene,  -thujene and  -pinene, which have antibacterial, antiviral, antifungal activities 3,  7,  8,  10,  12.
2: Due to its well-known properties,  S.hortensis essential oil has been imposed in various applicative fields, from the food industry to aromatherapy, which involves several treatments and heating processes that can affect its composition and thermal properties.
