0: Zingiberis Rhizoma Recens (ZRR) is the fresh rhizoma of  Zingiber officinale Roscoe and has a long history as an effective traditional Chinese medicine.
1: The use of ZRR can be traced from the  Shen Nong Ben Cao Jing.
2: In Chinese Pharmacopoeia, ZRR has the function of relieving exterior syndrome and dispersing cold and warming middle energizer to arrest vomiting.
3: It is mostly used to treat cold, vomiting, and cough caused by wind weather.
4: ZRR is distributed in China, India, Indonesia, Nigeria, and other countries and is famous for its antiemetic effect.
