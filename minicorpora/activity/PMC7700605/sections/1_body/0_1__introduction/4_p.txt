0: The aim of this study was to gather information on five medicinal plants from the Lamiaceae and Asteraceae family from Mt.
1: Rtanj and the village of Sesalac in Eastern Serbia, to determine their EO yield and composition and compare the results with literature data on the same species.
2: A second objective was to assess the antibacterial activity of these EOs against  Staphylococcus aureus and  Escherichia coli bacterial strains.
3: Traditionally, the five plant species have been used by Serbian traditional medicine for treatment of various health conditions and diseases as outlined above.
