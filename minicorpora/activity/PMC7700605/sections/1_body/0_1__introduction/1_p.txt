0: The plants of the Lamiaceae and Asteraceae families are widely distributed medicinal plants throughout the world and have been used since ancient times for medicine and food.
1: Historically, medicinal plants from these families have been used for flavoring, food preservation and medicinal purposes, due to their bioactive properties [ 1, 2, 3].
2: Today, pharmaceutical companies are interested in the wide range of beneficial properties of these plants in order to develop modern herbal remedies that would be used either as a replacement or supplement to conventional medicines and for prevention of illnesses [ 4, 5, 6].
3: In addition, medicinal plants are valuable raw material for perfumery and cosmetics, but also for healing purposes in self-medication [ 7, 8, 9], as well as in organic agriculture [ 10, 11, 12].
