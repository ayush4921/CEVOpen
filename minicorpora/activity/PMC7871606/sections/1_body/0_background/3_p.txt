0: In traditional medicine,  Rosmarinus officinalis L. (rosemary) uses as a treatment for asthma, renal colic pain, and dysmenorrhea.
1: The main components isolated from rosemary essential oil monoterpenes have anti-inflammatory, anti-oxidant, and anti-nociceptive effects [ 11].
2: Raskovic et al.
3: have demonstrated that rosemary essential oil can alleviate pain in the hot-plate test [ 12].
4: Also, Belkhodja et al.
5: have studied the effect of rosemary and white poplar essential oils in an animal model of knee osteoarthritis.
6: Radiographic and histologic data of this study have shown that the essential oil has a protective effect against osteoarthritis in rats [ 13].
7: Ghasemzadeh and colleagues have evaluated the anti-allodynic and anti-hyperalgesic effects of rosemary extract in rats with neuropathic pain.
8: This study also has demonstrated that rosemary extract has anti-inflammatory and anti-apoptotic effects that can treat neuropathic injury [ 14].
9: Besides, an in vitro study on bovine articular cartilage cells has revealed that rosemary extract slows down cartilage degeneration [ 15].
