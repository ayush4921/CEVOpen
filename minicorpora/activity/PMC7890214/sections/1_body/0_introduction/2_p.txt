0: Humankind is often vulnerable to oxidative stress due to reactive oxygen species (ROS) and reactive nitrogen species (RNS) ( Tanvir et al., 2017) as well as to diseases caused by bacterial infections ( Niamsa and Sittiwet, 2009).
1: Many people take antioxidants and antimicrobials in a form of commercial food additives that are produced synthetically and contain high amounts of preservatives to counter the effects of oxidative stress and growth of food spoiling microbes ( Sagdc and Ozcan, 2003;  Akter et al., 2019).
2: However, due to consumer health concerns related to the safety of food containing synthetic chemicals as preservatives, there is a growing interest in using natural antioxidants and antibacterial compounds ( Norajit et al., 2007;  Vallverdu-Queralt et al., 2015).
