A heatmap overview (showing only group average) with hierarchical clustering of the 4,774 cannabis oils. The first cluster (#1) included Bedrocan variety and the second one (#2) the other varieties, which in particular consisted of (#2A) Bedrolite and (#2B) Bediol and FM2. In respect to other varieties, Bedrocan displayed a lower concentration of CBD (tot, neutral, and acid) and Bedrolite of THC (tot and neutral). The color-scale differentiates values as high (red), mid (gray), and low (blue).
