0: Thuja trees belongs to the order Pineales, in the  Cupressaceae family [ 21].
1: The Thuja genus contains five species:  Thuja koraiensis, Nakai-Korean thuja;  Thuja occidentalis L., eastern arborvitae, northern white cedar;  Thuja plicata Donn ex D. Don, Western red cedar;  Thuja standishii (Gordon), Carriere-Japanese thuja; and  Thuja sutchuenensis, Franch-Sichuan thuja.
2: They are evergreens trees growing from 3 to 60 m tall with flattened branchlets, distributed in North America and East Asia.
