0: Family Lamiaceae or Labiatae popularly known as Mint family contains 236 genera and 69007000 species of aromatic plants [ 13].
1: Members of this family are herbs (perennial or annual herbs), shrubs, and trees.
2: Stems are herbaceous rarely woody, prostrate and branched.
3: Leaves are simple and oppositely arranged, rarely whorled, petiolate or sessile, hairy with characteristic aroma due to presence aromatic essential oils.
