0: The aim of this study is to determine and widen the fatty acid composition of tobacco oil grown in Almaty region by gas chromatography method and to find its potential uses in the preparation of food and perfumery because the use of tobacco seed oil in food and perfumes preparation has not been reported before.
1: The present research was conducted to investigate the feasibility of alternative uses of tobacco seeds for nutraceutical or functional food ingredients.
