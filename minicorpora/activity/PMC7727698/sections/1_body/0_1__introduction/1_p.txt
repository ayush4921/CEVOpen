0: In medical practice, infectious diseases are a major health problem and the development of antibiotic-resistant bacteria is a challenge nowadays.
1: In recent years there has been a growing interest in the use of natural products capable of replacing or being used to complement antibiotic treatment.
2: Due to their availability, absence of side effects or toxicity, better biodegradability compared to available antibiotics and preservatives, essential oils (EOs) have aroused the interest of researchers [ 1, 2].
3: EOs are complex natural mixtures of volatile secondary metabolites, isolated from plants by hydrodistillation.
4: They have been shown to be biocids against a wide range of organisms, such as bacteria, fungi, viruses, protozoa, insects and plants [ 3, 4, 5, 6].
