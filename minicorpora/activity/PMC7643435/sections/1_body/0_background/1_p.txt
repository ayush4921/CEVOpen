0: Antibiotic therapy is one of the most important therapies used for treating bacterial infections and has tremendously improved human life from the days of its introduction.
1: But in the past few decades, the development and spread of resistant pathogenic strains threatens human health globally [ 1].
2: The most important factor for developing resistance is the excessive exposure of bacterial strains to antibiotics [ 2].
3: Consequently, it is essential either to reduce/halt the use of antibiotics or to develop and use the alternative of antimicrobials.
